## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Why is Sri Venkateswara Guest House considered the best location?

It’s the closest TTD accommodation to the main temple – just 5-7 minutes walking distance, saving time in queue lines.

Q2: What is included in the ₹500 ordinary room?

Basic amenities: clean bed, attached bathroom with 24-hour water, fan, and cupboard. No TV or AC in ordinary rooms.

Q3: Can unmarried couples book rooms?

No, TTD strictly allows only married couples with valid marriage proof. Unrelated adults of opposite gender cannot share rooms.

Q4: Is there 24-hour hot water available?

Yes, all rooms have 24-hour hot water supply. However, pressure may vary during peak hours.

Q5: How early should I reach for check-in?

Check-in starts at 12:00 PM. Arrive within 2 hours of your check-in time to avoid cancellation.

Q6: Are luggage storage facilities available?

Yes, cloakroom facilities are available at reception for storing luggage before check-in or after check-out.

Q7: Can I book for a large family group?

Yes, multiple rooms can be booked but each room requires separate booking with different ID proofs.

Q8: What if I miss my online booking check-in time?

Your booking may be cancelled. Contact reception immediately. Rooms are held only for 2 hours beyond check-in time.

Q9: Are there any medical facilities nearby?

TTD’s Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences (SVIMS) is nearby. Basic first aid is available at reception.

Q10: Can I get room service or housekeeping?

Basic housekeeping is provided once daily. No room service or laundry facilities are available.

Q11: Is WiFi available for guests?

Limited WiFi may be available in common areas but not guaranteed in rooms due to temple area restrictions.

Q12: What payment methods are accepted for online booking?

All major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and mobile wallets are accepted on the TTD portal.
