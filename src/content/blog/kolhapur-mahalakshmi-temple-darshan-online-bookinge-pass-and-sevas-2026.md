The Shri Mahalakshmi Temple in Kolhapur, also known as the Ambabai Temple, is one of India’s most revered spiritual destinations. As one of the 18 Maha Shakti Peethas, it is believed to be where the purest form of divine energy resides. This guide provides all the essential information for a fulfilling pilgrimage, including the latest online booking process for 2026, temple timings, and seva details.

## About Kolhapur Mahalakshmi Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the deity of wealth, prosperity, and fortune, this ancient temple dates back to the 7th century CE during the Chalukya dynasty. Located in the heart of Kolhapur city, it is a cornerstone of spiritual heritage in Maharashtra. A pilgrimage to this temple, combined with Tirumala and the Padmavathi Temple, is considered highly auspicious for achieving prosperity and inner peace.

## Kolhapur Mahalakshmi Temple Online Darshan E-Pass Booking (Step-by-Step)

Booking an E-Pass is essential for a smooth visit, especially during festivals. Follow these steps using the official temple website.

Step| Action to Take  
---|---  
**1\. Visit Official Site**|  Go to **[mahalaxmikolhapur.com](https://mahalaxmikolhapur.com/)** (the only authorized portal).  
**2\. Select Service**|  Click on the **“E-Pass / Online Darshan”** option on the homepage.  
**3\. Choose Slot**|  Select your preferred visit date and available time slot.  
**4\. Enter Details**|  Fill in personal details (Name, Age, Contact) and upload a valid ID proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID).  
**5\. Review & Submit**| Double-check all information and click ‘Submit’.  
**6\. Download E-Pass**|  Download the PDF E-Pass containing a unique QR code.  
**7\. Carry to Temple**|  Bring a printed copy or digital copy on your phone, along with the original ID used for booking.  
**8\. Arrive Early**|  Reach the temple at least 15–20 minutes before your scheduled slot for verification.

## Travel Tips and Accommodation Guide

  * **Dress Code:** Wear modest, traditional attire as a mark of respect.
  * **What to Carry:** A valid photo ID for verification. Avoid large bags and valuables.
  * **For Special Assistance:** Wheelchairs and help are available for senior citizens and differently-abled devotees.
  * **Accommodation:** The **Mahalaxmi Temple Dharamshala** offers basic, affordable rooms on a first-come, first-served basis at the temple counter. Many budget and mid-range hotels are also available nearby in Kolhapur city.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: How can I book Kolhapur Mahalakshmi Temple Darshan online?**  
A: Book only through the official website, **[mahalaxmikolhapur.com](https://mahalaxmikolhapur.com/)** , by selecting your date, time slot, and providing required details.

**Q2: Is an E-Pass mandatory for darshan?**  
A: It is highly recommended, especially on weekends and during festivals, to ensure entry and avoid long queues.

**Q3: What are the general temple opening hours?**  
A: The temple opens at 4:00 AM and closes at 10:30 PM daily, with specific rituals throughout the day.

**Q4: Can I perform a seva on behalf of someone else?**  
A: Yes, you can book sevas in another person’s name by providing their details during the online booking process.

**Q5: How do I reach the temple?**  
A: Kolhapur is well-connected by road, rail, and air. The temple is centrally located in the city, about 5 km from Kolhapur Junction railway station.

**Q6: Is photography allowed inside the temple?**  
A: Photography is strictly prohibited inside the sanctum sanctorum. It may be allowed in certain outer areas only with prior permission.

**Q7: What is the best time for a peaceful darshan?**  
A: The early morning hours between 4:00 AM to 8:00 AM are the most serene and peaceful for darshan.

**Q8: Where can I check for live temple updates?**  
A: All official announcements and festival details are posted on the temple’s official website, **[mahalaxmikolhapur.com](https://mahalaxmikolhapur.com/)**.
