Dreaming of participating in the divine swing ritual where Lord Venkateswara and Goddess Padmavathi are gently rocked on a golden cradle? **Unjal Seva** (also called Dolotsavam or Swing Seva) at Tirumala is a beautiful evening Arjitha Seva where the utsava deities are placed on a swing in the Ayna Mahal (Mirror Hall) and swayed rhythmically amid Vedic chants, music, and flower offerings. This joyful seva symbolizes the Lord’s playful, loving nature and brings immense peace, marital harmony, and blessings for devotees. It’s one of the most sought-after daily sevas at Sri Venkateswara Temple, managed by **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)**.

### What is Unjal Seva (ఊంజల్ సేవ)?

This seva involves:

  * Placing the processional idols (Utsava Murthis) on a decorated swing.
  * Priests swinging it gently while reciting slokas and playing instruments.
  * Devotees witnessing from close proximity (limited participation).
  * Duration: About 30–60 minutes, performed in the evening for a serene experience.

It’s grouped with other afternoon/evening sevas like Kalyanotsavam, Arjitha Brahmotsavam, and Vasantotsavam in the same slot.

### Benefits of Participating

  * Marital bliss and harmony (ideal for couples)
  * Removal of obstacles and fulfillment of wishes
  * Divine grace for family peace and prosperity
  * Close, intimate darshan with special prasadam

Highly recommended for newlyweds or those seeking relationship blessings.

### Ticket Cost and What’s Included

  * **Cost** : Rs. 300 per person (as per recent TTD updates; some sources mention Rs. 200–500 — confirm on portal).
  * **Persons Allowed** : Usually 1–2 per ticket (limited slots; children under 12 may join free or with adult).
  * **Inclusions** : Witness the seva, special darshan during/after, prasadam (laddu/vada or similar).
  * **Note** : Part of Arjitha Seva quota; often bundled in releases with Kalyanotsavam, etc.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Seva Time** : 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM slot (Unjal Seva typically 1:30 PM – 2:30 PM or 5:00 PM – 6:00 PM; varies daily — check exact in day schedule).
  * **Reporting** : Arrive 30–60 minutes early at Supatham entrance or specified counter.
  * **Performed Daily** : Yes, throughout the year (subject to temple schedule).
  * **Dress Code** : Traditional only — men: dhoti/kurta with upper cloth; women: saree/salwar with dupatta (no jeans/T-shirts).

### How to Book Unjal Seva Online in 2026

Booking is **online only** through the official TTD portal (advance quotas release monthly; limited per user).

  1. Visit **ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in** (or TTD app).
  2. Register/login with mobile/Aadhaar.
  3. Go to Srivari Sevas → Arjitha Sevas.
  4. Select Unjal Seva (often under Kalyanotsavam/Unjal group) and preferred date.
  5. Enter participant details (limited persons; ID required).
  6. Pay online and download e-ticket (print/digital mandatory).

  * **Quota Release** : Monthly (e.g., May 2026 released Feb 2026 at 10:00 AM; check TTD announcements for exact dates like Feb 21 or 24 for upcoming months).
  * **Advance** : Up to 120 days; electronic dip/lucky dip for some; book early as slots sell fast.
  * **Maximum** : 1–2 tickets per login; one seva per user in 180 days rule applies.
  * **Current/Same-Day** : Rare; mostly advance.

Report with ticket and ID; group together.

### Important Rules and Tips

  * Traditional dress mandatory; report early for verification.
  * No mobiles/cameras inside; deposit at cloak room.
  * Combine with darshan (special entry often allowed post-seva).
  * High demand — book right at quota release (10:00 AM sharp).
  * Plan Tirumala stay (book TTD rooms online simultaneously).
  * For queries: TTD helpline or official site.

This enchanting seva offers a tender, joyful connection with Lord Balaji.
