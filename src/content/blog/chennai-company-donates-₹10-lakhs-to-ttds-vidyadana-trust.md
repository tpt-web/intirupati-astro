Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) has received a generous donation of **₹10 Lakhs (Ten Lakh Rupees)** from a corporate donor. The contribution was made to support the trust’s charitable and educational activities.

### 🤝 **Details of the Donation**

  * **Donor** : **Dhara Logistics** , a company based in Chennai.
  * **Beneficiary Trust** : **TTD Sri Venkateswara Vidyadana Trust**. This trust is dedicated to supporting education and knowledge-based initiatives.
  * **Date of Handover** : Wednesday, January 14, 2026.
  * **Location** : The Additional EO Camp Office in Tirumala.

### 🎯 **Purpose of the Donation**

The donation was made to the **Sri Venkateswara Vidyadana Trust**. “Vidyadana” translates to “the donation of knowledge” and is considered one of the greatest forms of charity. Donations to this specific trust are utilized by TTD for:

  * Promoting educational programs and initiatives.
  * Supporting students and educational institutions.
  * Furthering knowledge-based services as part of TTD’s philanthropic mission.

### 👥 **Officials Present**

The donation was formally handed over in the presence of senior TTD officials, ensuring transparency and official acknowledgment:

  * **Sri Ch. Venkaiah Chowdary** , Additional Executive Officer (EO), TTD.
  * **Sri Bhanu Prakash Reddy** , Member of the TTD Board.

Corporate and individual donations like this play a crucial role in helping TTD sustain and expand its wide range of social, religious, and charitable services for the benefit of devotees and society.
