### **Srivari Seva: Serve with Love at the Abode of Lord Venkateswara**

Have you ever felt a calling to serve that transcends ordinary acts? At the heart of Hindu Dharma lies the beautiful ideal: **“Manava Seva is Madhava Seva”** – Service to Humanity is Service to the Divine. Inspired by this eternal truth, the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) offers you a sacred pathway to express your devotion: **Srivari Seva**.

This voluntary service is a living tradition where devotees become the helping hands for lakhs of fellow pilgrims visiting the holy hills of Tirumala. It’s more than a duty; it’s a spiritual practice that enriches the soul by serving the Lord in every devotee.

### **What is Srivari Seva?**

Srivari Seva is TTD’s organized volunteer program that began in the year 2000. From a humble beginning of 200 volunteers, it has now grown into a mighty family of over **13 lakh Sevaks** from across India and the world.

  * **The Essence** : To selflessly serve pilgrims, ensuring their journey to see Lord Venkateswara is comfortable and peaceful.
  * **The Spirit** : It is a free service offered with love and devotion, following in the footsteps of great Acharyas.
  * **The Scale** : On any given day, 2,000 to 3,500 Sevaks are actively serving in various temple zones.

### Srivari Seva **Eligibility & How to Join**

To maintain the sanctity and efficiency of the service, TTD has set clear guidelines for volunteers.

Eligibility Criteria| Details  
---|---  
**Age Limit**|  18 to 60 years  
**Health**|  Physically strong and mentally fit for sustained service  
**Religion**|  Must belong to the Hindu faith  
**Background**|  Must have no criminal record or pending cases  
**Service Nature**| **100% Voluntary** – No monetary payment is provided  
**Important Note**|  Children or elderly dependents cannot accompany Sevaks during service.  
  
**The Registration Process is Simple:**

  1. Visit the official **TTD Srivari Seva Online Portal**.
  2. Fill in the required details to register as an individual or team.
  3. Once registered, you can select available service slots for your pilgrimage.

### **Seva Guidelines & Code of Conduct**

Adherence to these instructions ensures a smooth, respectful, and spiritually aligned service experience for everyone.

#### **Key Instructions for Sevaks**

  * **Reporting** : Bring the original ID proof used for online registration. Team leaders must bring the entire group.
  * **Accommodation** : TTD provides **separate dormitory-style housing** (Sadan-1 for women, Sadan-2 for men). Bring your own lock.
  * **Duty** : Minimum **6 hours of service per day** in allotted areas.
  * **Uniform – Strictly Mandatory** : 
    * **Men** : White shirt & white pant/dhoti/kurta-pyjama.
    * **Women** : Orange saree with maroon border & blouse, or orange top with maroon bottom and dupatta.
    * All must wear the provided **Srivari Seva scarf** while on duty and apply **Tirunamam** (sacred mark).

#### **Sacred Code of Conduct**

  * Serve every pilgrim as Lord himself, addressing all as **“Govinda”** with patience and affection.
  * Maintain high standards of integrity, courtesy, and discipline.
  * **Strictly Prohibited** : Consumption of alcohol, tobacco, or non-vegetarian food; gambling; any form of discrimination; arguing with staff or pilgrims; proselytization.
  * **Important** : Do not pay anyone for temple duties. Allotment is electronic. Evading duty for personal darshan can lead to a 2-year ban from service.

### **Frequently Asked Questions**

Question| Answer  
---|---  
**What is the core idea behind Srivari Seva?**|  It embodies “Service to devotees is service to God,” allowing you to serve the Lord by helping His pilgrims.  
**Is there a salary or stipend?**|  No. This is **Nishkamya Seva** (selfless service) offered purely as devotional surrender.  
**Where will I be serving?**|  Sevaks are assigned to various public areas like queue lines, annadanam halls, luggage counters, and pilgrim assistance points.  
**What is Parakamani Seva?**|  It is a specific, advanced seva involving the counting of Hundi offerings. Registration for it is announced separately by TTD.  
**How can I ensure my registration is smooth?**|  Read all instructions on the portal carefully, ensure you meet the criteria, and have your ID documents ready.
