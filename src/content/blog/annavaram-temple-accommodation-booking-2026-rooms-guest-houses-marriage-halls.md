## Annavaram Temple Accommodation Booking Methods

### Method 1: Walk-in Booking at CRO (Current Booking)

**CRO = Central Registration Office**

**Location:** Ratnagiri Hill, near main temple entrance

**Office Hours:** **24 hours** (yes, round-the-clock service!)

**Procedure:**

  1. **Arrive at Annavaram** and proceed to CRO office
  2. **Enquire about room availability** in various guest houses
  3. **Select preferred room type** based on availability and budget
  4. **Submit documents:**
     * Photocopy of any **Photo ID** (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Driving License)
     * Provide details: Name, mobile number, address
  5. **Pay room rent** \+ **refundable security deposit** (usually ₹500-₹1,000)
  6. **Receive room key and receipt**
  7. **Check-in** at allotted guest house
  8. **Check-out:** Return key, get security refunded

**Advantages:**

  * No advance booking hassle
  * See room before booking
  * Flexible same-day planning
  * Available 24 hours

**Disadvantages:**

  * No guarantee of availability during peak times
  * May get downhill rooms if uphill full
  * Popular rooms (Prakash Sadan) fill quickly on weekends

**Best Timing for Walk-in:**

  * **Early morning (5:00-7:00 AM):** Best availability
  * **Late night (after 8:00 PM):** Cancellations available
  * **Avoid:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM on weekends (peak crowd)

### Method 2: MeeSeva/CSC Centers Booking (Advance – 30 Days)

**MeeSeva** = Government e-governance initiative for citizen services

**Advance Booking Period:** **30 days before** intended stay

**Availability:** Most guest houses (except Prakash Sadan)

**How to Book via MeeSeva:**

**Step 1: Locate MeeSeva Center**

  * Find nearest **MeeSeva/CSC/eSeva center** in Andhra Pradesh or Telangana
  * Available in most towns and cities
  * Search online: “MeeSeva center near me”

**Step 2: Visit Center with Documents**

  * **Photo ID** (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID/Driving License)
  * Mobile number
  * Email ID (optional)

**Step 3: Provide Booking Details**

  * **Temple Name:** Sri Veera Venkata Satyanarayana Swamy Temple, Annavaram
  * **Guest House Preference:** Hari Hara Sadan, Satyadeva, Centenary, etc.
  * **Check-in Date:** Exact date (within 30 days)
  * **Number of Rooms:** How many needed
  * **Number of Persons:** Total occupants
  * **Duration:** Number of days

**Step 4: Payment**

  * Pay **50% advance** \+ one day rent
  * Example: For ₹400/day room for 2 days = ₹400 + (₹400 × 0.5) = ₹600 advance
  * Payment: Cash/Card at MeeSeva center
  * **Service Charge:** ₹20-₹30 (MeeSeva processing fee)

**Step 5: Receive Confirmation**

  * Get **printed receipt** with booking ID
  * **SMS confirmation** to mobile
  * Save receipt (mandatory to carry to temple)

**Step 6: Check-in at Temple**

  * Show booking receipt at CRO office
  * Pay remaining 50% + security deposit
  * Receive room key

**MeeSeva Booking Advantages:**

  * Guaranteed room 30 days advance
  * Secure booking
  * No last-minute stress
  * Good for weekend/festival visits

**MeeSeva Limitations:**

  * **Prakash Sadan NOT available** via MeeSeva (only walk-in)
  * Booking exactly 30 days before (not earlier)
  * Must visit physical MeeSeva center
  * **NO online MeeSeva booking** currently for Annavaram

**Important:** Unlike other temples, Annavaram does NOT have direct online accommodation booking through its website. MeeSeva requires physical visit to center.

### Method 3: Demand Draft (DD) Booking

**For Devotees Outside AP/Telangana** or those unable to visit MeeSeva

**Advance Booking:** **30 days before** stay

**Procedure:**

**Step 1: Prepare DD**

  * Purchase **Demand Draft** from any bank
  * **Amount:** One day rent + 50% of total rent 
    * Example: 2-day stay in ₹400 room = ₹400 + (₹800 × 0.5) = ₹800

## Room Allotment Rules & Policies

### General Rules

  1. **Minimum Occupancy:** Rooms generally not allotted for **single person** (security reasons) 
     * Exceptions: Married couples, families, same-gender groups
  2. **Maximum Stay:** **2-3 days maximum** (may vary during non-peak times)
  3. **Check-in Time:** **Flexible** – Can check in at any time (24-hour CRO)
  4. **Check-out Time:** **24 hours from check-in** or by **10:00 AM next day** (varies by guest house)
  5. **ID Proof:** **Mandatory** for all guests (photocopy required)
  6. **Security Deposit:** ₹500-₹1,000 (refundable on checkout)
  7. **Room Capacity:**
     * Single Room: 2 persons (1 cot)
     * Double Room: 4 persons (2 cots)
     * Extra bedding may be provided for children

### Cancellation Policy

**MeeSeva/DD Bookings:**

  * **No clear refund policy** mentioned
  * **Cancellation difficult** once booked
  * Write to Executive Officer with reason
  * Refund at temple’s discretion

**Walk-in Bookings:**

  * **Early checkout:** Get refund for unused days + full security
  * **Full security refund:** If room in good condition

### Priority Allotment

**Preference Given To:**

  * Senior citizens (65+ years)
  * Physically challenged
  * Pregnant women
  * Parents with infants
  * Couples performing vratham/kalyanam

**How to Request Priority:**

  * Mention at CRO counter
  * Show relevant proof (age, medical certificate)
  * Subject to availability

### Special Accommodations

**For Elderly/Disabled:**

  * Ground floor rooms preferred
  * Rooms closer to temple
  * Wheelchair access (limited)
  * Assistance from staff (on request)

**For Families with Children:**

  * Extra mattress/bedding provided
  * Rooms near CRO (safer)
  * Preference for downhill (less climbing)

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1\. Can I book Annavaram Temple rooms online?

**Currently NO direct online booking** through temple website. You can book via **MeeSeva centers** (physical visit) or **DD method** (postal) for 30-day advance, or **walk-in at CRO** (24 hours).

### 2\. What is the price range for Annavaram accommodation?

**₹200 to ₹3,000 per day.** Basic choultries: ₹200. Standard rooms: ₹400-₹600. AC rooms: ₹950-₹1,050. Suite rooms: ₹1,500-₹3,000.

### 3\. Can I book Prakash Sadan in advance?

**NO.** Prakash Sadan is **ONLY walk-in booking** at CRO counter. Not available via MeeSeva or DD. Arrive very early morning (5 AM) for best chance.

### 4\. What is MeeSeva booking for Annavaram?

**MeeSeva** is government e-governance center. Visit **any MeeSeva/CSC center** in AP/Telangana to book rooms **30 days in advance** (except Prakash Sadan). Pay 50% advance + one day rent.

### 5\. How to book rooms via DD method?

Send **Demand Draft** (1 day rent + 50% total rent) payable to “**Executive Officer, Annavaram Devasthanam** ” with requisition letter (details: name, dates, guest house preference) to temple postal address.

### 6\. What is CRO office at Annavaram?

**CRO = Central Registration Office.** Located on Ratnagiri Hill near temple. **Open 24 hours.** Handles walk-in room bookings, check-in, check-out, and all accommodation queries.

### 7\. Which is the best guest house near Annavaram Temple?

**Prakash Sadan** (closest – 50m from temple, ₹650-₹1,600). **Hari Hara Sadan** (5min walk, ₹400-₹950, advance booking available). **Satyadeva** (₹400, budget-friendly, 8min walk).

### 8\. Hari Hara Sadan Annavaram contact number?

**Temple main numbers:** 08868-238121, 238125, 238163. **Accommodation helpline:** +91-9393939150. **CRO office:** Visit in person on Ratnagiri Hill (24 hours).

### 9\. What is Prakash Sadan to Annavaram temple distance?

**Only 50 meters** (approximately 2-3 minutes walk). Prakash Sadan is the **closest accommodation** to the main temple entrance.

### 10\. How to book marriage hall at Annavaram Temple?

Contact temple **30 days before** marriage date. Submit: Bride/groom IDs, parents’ IDs, Sumuhurtham paper. Pay **1.5x hall rent** as advance. **2 rooms provided** complementary. Rent: ₹700-₹50,000.

### 11\. Are Annavaram Temple rooms available today?

**Walk-in always available at CRO** (24 hours), but during peak times (weekends/festivals/Ekadasi), uphill rooms may fill. Call **+91-9393939150** to check current status before traveling.

### 12\. What documents needed for room booking?

**Photo ID proof** (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID/Driving License) – photocopy required. Provide: name, mobile number, address. If MeeSeva/DD: booking receipt. That’s it!

### 13\. How much is security deposit for rooms?

Usually **₹500-₹1,000** refundable security deposit. Fully refunded at checkout if room in good condition. Paid at check-in along with room rent.

### 14\. Can I get refund if I checkout early?

**YES.** If you check out early, you get **refund for unused days** \+ full security deposit. Example: Booked 2 days, stayed 1 day = get 1 day rent back.

### 15\. Are rooms available for single person?

Generally **NO** (security policy). Exceptions: With special permission, solo spiritual pilgrims, same-gender dormitory option (₹200). Best to travel with at least one companion.

### 16\. Check-in and check-out timings?

**Check-in:** Flexible (24-hour CRO). Can check-in anytime. **Check-out:** Within 24 hours of check-in OR by 10:00 AM next day (varies by guest house).

### 17\. How many days maximum can I stay?

Generally **2-3 days maximum** during peak seasons. During off-season with low crowd, longer stays may be permitted (ask at CRO).

### 18\. Are there AC rooms at Annavaram Temple?

**YES.** Prakash Sadan AC: ₹1,050 (44 rooms). Prakash Sadan Double AC: ₹1,600 (1 room). Hari Hara Sadan AC: ₹950 (84 rooms – largest AC availability).

### 19\. Which guest house can I book via MeeSeva?

**Most guest houses EXCEPT Prakash Sadan.** Can book via MeeSeva: Hari Hara Sadan, Satyadeva, Centenary Cottages, Seetha Rama Choultry, Vana Durga, Satya Niketan, etc.

### 20\. How early should I reach for walk-in booking?

For **Prakash Sadan:** 5:00-6:00 AM (best chance). For **other guest houses:** 7:00-8:00 AM usually fine on weekdays. Weekends/festivals: Earlier the better.

### 21\. Are marriage halls available at Annavaram?

**YES.** 18+ halls available (₹700-₹50,000/day). Largest: Sri Satya Srinivasa A/C Mandapam (1000+ capacity, ₹50,000). Book 30 days advance. 2 rooms provided free.

### 22\. Does temple provide priests for marriage?

**NO.** Temple provides only the hall/space. You must arrange: **Purohit (priest), decorations, catering, bhajan group, photography** – all separately. Temple-approved vendor list available.

### 23\. Can we conduct other events besides marriages?

**YES.** Namakarana (naming ceremony), Upanayanam (thread ceremony), Shashtipoorthi, Satyanarayan Vratham for groups. Small halls (₹700-₹5,000) ideal for family events.

### 24\. Are there vegetarian food facilities?

**Annadanam** (free meals) provided by temple at specific times. **Canteens** available near temple (₹50-₹150 per meal). **Private restaurants** in Annavaram town. **Only vegetarian food** allowed in temple premises.

### 25\. What if all uphill rooms are full?

Accept **downhill accommodation** (Satya Niketan ₹200, Pampa Cottage ₹200). You’ll need to **climb 460 steps daily** for temple visit OR use **vehicular road** (auto/temple shuttle available).
