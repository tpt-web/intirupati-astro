Marriage is one of the most sacred milestones in Hindu tradition. To bless newly married couples as they begin their life journey together, **Sri Venkateswara Swamy of Tirumala** offers divine blessings known as **Srivari Akshintalu**.

This special initiative by **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)** allows newlyweds to receive sacred prasadam and blessings directly from Tirumala, even if they are unable to visit the temple immediately after marriage.

## What Are Srivari Akshintalu?

**Srivari Akshintalu** are sacred rice grains (talambralu) blessed by Lord Venkateswara. These are sent to newly married couples as part of the **Srivari Aseervadam** program.

The blessings symbolize:

  * A happy and harmonious married life
  * Prosperity and mutual understanding
  * Divine protection and spiritual strength

Lakhs of couples across India receive these blessings every year.

## Spiritual Importance for Newly Married Couples

In Hindu culture, marriage is not just a social bond but a spiritual commitment. Seeking Lord Venkateswara’s blessings at the beginning of married life is believed to bring peace, stability, and long-lasting happiness.

Srivari Akshintalu represent:

  * Divine approval of the marriage
  * Blessings for family growth
  * Strength to face life’s responsibilities together

## What Is Included in the Srivari Akshintalu Package?

Newlyweds usually receive the following sacred items from Tirumala:

  * **Akshintalu (Blessed Rice):** Used during auspicious rituals for prosperity
  * **Kankanam & Kumkum:** Symbol of protection and marital well-being
  * **Blessing Letter:** Issued by TTD with divine wishes
  * **Spiritual Booklet:** Explains values of married life and dharma
  * **Images of Sri Venkateswara and Padmavathi Ammavaru**

Each item carries deep spiritual significance.

## How to Apply for Tirumala Srivari Akshintalu

The process is **simple and completely free**.

### Steps to Follow:

  1. Take a copy of your **wedding invitation card**
  2. Write a covering note with your **full postal address**
  3. Send it by post to the address below

### Postal Address:

The Executive Officer  
Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)  
TTD Administrative Building  
K.T. Road, Tirupati – 517501  
Andhra Pradesh, India

## Important Guidelines for Couples

  * Send the invitation **before or soon after marriage**
  * Ensure the **address is clear and complete**
  * There is **no online application** for this service
  * Delivery may take **a few weeks** , depending on postal service

## Is There Any Fee for Srivari Akshintalu?

**No.**  
  
The entire process is a **free divine service** offered by TTD to bless newly married couples.

Receiving **Srivari Akshintalu from Tirumala** is a deeply meaningful experience for newlyweds. It connects the couple spiritually with Lord Venkateswara and brings divine grace into their married life.

If you are recently married or planning your wedding, make sure to apply and receive this sacred blessing.
