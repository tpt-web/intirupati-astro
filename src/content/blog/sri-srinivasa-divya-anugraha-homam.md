Seeking the divine grace of Lord Venkateswara without the long Tirumala queues? **Sri Srinivasa Divya Anugraha Visesha Homam** (also called Divyanugraha Homam) is a popular daily ritual in Tirupati that offers spiritual blessings and a convenient way to get **Special Entry Darshan** at Tirumala. Managed by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD), this homam is perfect for devotees wanting prosperity, health, and obstacle removal under Lord Srinivasa’s blessings.

### What is Sri Srinivasa Divya Anugraha Homam?

This is a special fire ritual (homam) performed daily according to Vaikhanasa Agama traditions by priests from Sri Venkateswara Vedic University. It involves sacred offerings, mantra chanting (including Panchasuktam, Ashtothara, and more), and invocations to Lord Venkateswara. Held at the base of the hills, it allows participants to receive divine anugraha (blessings) and includes perks like special darshan. It’s a great option for couples, friends, families, or singles to participate and seek the Lord’s grace.

### Benefits of Participating

Devotees believe this homam brings:

  * Removal of life obstacles and hurdles
  * Wealth, prosperity, and financial stability
  * Good health and well-being
  * Success in endeavors and career
  * Harmony in relationships
  * Protection from negative energies Plus, you get Special Entry Darshan at Tirumala (usually Rs. 300 value) on the same day, making it a cost-effective spiritual experience.

### Ticket Price and What’s Included

  * **Cost** : Rs. 1600 per ticket (valid for 2 persons). Some sources mention Rs. 1000 earlier, but current 2026 updates confirm Rs. 1600, which includes the homam and Special Entry Darshan benefit for both participants.
  * Not limited to couples — 2 males, 2 females, or any combination allowed.
  * Children under 12 often free (check during booking).
  * No separate darshan ticket needed; it’s bundled.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Reporting Time** : 8:30 AM (arrive early for smooth entry).
  * **Homam Starts** : 9:00 AM and lasts 2–2.5 hours (ends around 11:00–11:30 AM).
  * **Special Darshan** : At Tirumala around 3:00 PM on the same day (via special queue).
  * Performed **daily** throughout the year at Saptha Gau Pradhakshina Shala, Alipiri (footpath starting point in Tirupati). Dress code: Traditional attire only (men: dhoti + shirt/upper cloth; women: saree/salwar with dupatta/pallu). No jeans, T-shirts, or modern wear.

### Location

The homam takes place at **Saptha Gau Pradhakshina Shala (Sapta Go Pradakshina Mandiram), Alipiri** , Tirupati — near the starting point of the Alipiri walkway to Tirumala. After the homam, head to Tirumala for darshan (transport via bus or steps).

### How to Book Online for 2026

Booking is **online only** through the official TTD portal (no offline counter since mid-2025). Slots are limited and sell out fast!

  1. Visit **ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in** (official TTD site) or use the TTD app.
  2. Register/login with your details (Aadhaar-linked).
  3. Go to Srivari Sevas → Homams → Sri Srinivasa Divyanugraha Visesha Homam.
  4. Select your preferred date in the month.
  5. Enter participant details (names, IDs — Aadhaar required).
  6. Pay online and download the ticket (print it).

**Ticket Release Dates** : Quota opens about one month in advance, usually around the 25th–27th at 10:00 AM.

  * For **February 2026** : Released January 27, 2026, at 10:00 AM (already passed as of now).
  * For **March 2026** : Typically around February 25, 2026, at 10:00 AM (check official site for exact).
  * General pattern: Previous month’s end (e.g., Jan quota in Dec). Book early on release day — use multiple logins if needed (one ticket per mobile often). Maximum: Usually 1–2 tickets per user per booking; one seva per user in 180 days.

Bring printed ticket and original ID (Aadhaar/passport) for verification. Report together as a group.

### Important Tips

  * High demand — book right at release time.
  * No name changes allowed post-booking.
  * No accommodation included; book separately via TTD (or private stays in Tirupati).
  * Participate fully to avail darshan benefit.
  * For latest updates, check official TTD site or news.tirumala.org.
