## 📱 How to Book Madhavam Guest House Online

Madhavam Guest House accepts **online bookings only** through the official TTD website. Here’s your complete step-by-step guide:

### Step-by-Step Online Booking Process:

1

#### Visit TTD Official Portal

Go to **ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in** and click on “Accommodation” section. You must have a registered account to proceed.

2

#### Select Madhavam Guest House

Choose “Madhavam Guest House” from the list of available accommodations. The system will show available dates and room types.

3

#### Choose Check-in Time Slot

**CRITICAL STEP:** Select your check-in time slot carefully from the four available slots. You must report ONLY during your selected time:

  * **Slot 1:** 12:00 AM to 5:59 AM
  * **Slot 2:** 6:00 AM to 11:59 AM
  * **Slot 3:** 12:00 PM to 5:59 PM
  * **Slot 4:** 6:00 PM to 11:59 PM

4

#### Complete Payment

Make payment using credit/debit card, net banking, or UPI. The system will generate your booking confirmation.

5

#### Save Confirmation

Download and print your booking receipt. You’ll need this document along with original ID proof during check-in.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT ONLINE BOOKING RULES:**

  * **Strict Time Slot:** You must check-in ONLY during your selected time slot
  * **24-Hour Stay:** Room is allotted for exactly 24 hours from check-in time
  * **Online Only:** No walk-in or current booking facility available
  * **Advance Booking:** Book up to 90 days in advance (subject to availability)
  * **Cancellation Policy:** Only base tariff refunded (GST not refunded)
  * **Caution Deposit:** Refunded to your account within 7-10 days after checkout

## ⏰ Check-in & Check-out Procedure

#### Check-in Process

  1. Arrive at Madhavam Guest House during your selected time slot
  2. Present your booking confirmation receipt (printed copy)
  3. Show original ID proof matching booking details
  4. Complete registration at reception
  5. Collect room keys and caution deposit receipt

#### Check-out Process

  1. Vacate room before 24-hour completion time
  2. Inform reception of departure
  3. Return room keys
  4. Collect caution deposit acknowledgment
  5. Deposit refunded to original payment method in 7-10 days

**🚫 Late Check-in Consequences:** If you fail to check-in during your selected time slot, your booking will be cancelled automatically and the room will be allotted to waiting list pilgrims. No refunds for missed time slots!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book Madhavam Guest House without online booking?**

**No.** Madhavam Guest House accepts **online bookings only** through ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. No walk-in, current booking, or counter booking facilities are available.

**Q: What happens if I miss my check-in time slot?**

Your booking will be **automatically cancelled** and the room will be allotted to waiting list pilgrims. You will not receive a refund for missed time slots.

**Q: Are all rooms at Madhavam air-conditioned?**

**Yes.** Madhavam Guest House offers exclusively AC rooms. Two categories are available: A/C Ordinary Suite (₹800) and A/C Deluxe Suite (₹1,000).

**Q: How early can I book Madhavam rooms online?**

You can book **up to 90 days in advance** through the TTD online portal. Weekend and festival dates get booked quickly, so book as early as possible.

**Q: Is parking available at Madhavam?**

**Yes.** Ample parking space is available for both cars and two-wheelers. The parking area is secure and within the complex premises.

**Q: Can I cancel my booking and get a refund?**

Yes, but only the **base tariff** will be refunded. The GST component paid during booking is **not refundable**.

**Q: What facilities are shared with Srinivasam Complex?**

Madhavam guests can use Srinivasam Complex facilities including bank counters, medical dispensary, toiletries shop, and information center.
