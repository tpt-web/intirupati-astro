**Angapradakshinam** (also called **Anga Pradakshina**) at **Tirumala Tirupati** is a sacred vow where devotees roll their body around the inner **Vimana Pradakshinam** path near Lord Venkateswara’s sanctum. It shows complete surrender, humility, and gratitude to Lord Balaji for fulfilled wishes like health, family peace, or success.

TTD provides **limited free tokens** daily. Advance online booking is now the main way — no current offline or same-day tickets at Tirumala. This guide covers all: **Angapradakshinam for locals** , **tickets** , **benefits** , **timings** , **entry** , **offline options** (limited), and more for devotees from Nellore, Tirupati, or Andhra Pradesh.

### What is Angapradakshinam & Its Benefits?

Angapradakshinam means rolling the body (anga) in circumambulation (pradakshinam) around the holy path inside the temple. Devotees chant “Govinda” while doing one full roll with help from Srivari Sevaks.

**Spiritual Benefits & Significance**:

  * Shows total surrender (Sharanagati) to Lord Venkateswara.
  * Purifies the soul and removes bad karma from past lives.
  * Fulfills vows (mokku) for wishes like good health, job, marriage, or family problems.
  * Brings deep peace, divine blessings, and spiritual transformation.
  * Many devotees say it removes obstacles and grants moksha (liberation) when done with true devotion.

It is a rare and powerful seva — only a few get this chance daily.

### Angapradakshinam Timings & Darshan Timings

  * **Ritual Performance** : Early morning — report by 1:00 AM (or 12:30 AM on normal days; 1:00 AM during busy months like Peratasi). Gates open around 3:00 AM (women first, then men after Suprabhatam).
  * **Anga Pradakshina Timings** : Starts after Suprabhatam; one full roll per devotee.
  * **Darshan After Ritual** : Direct darshan of Lord Venkateswara + small laddu prasadam.
  * **Not Allowed On** : Fridays (Abhishekam) and major festivals (Brahmotsavam, Vaikuntha Ekadashi, etc.).
  * **Reporting Place** : Vaikuntam Queue Complex-1 (VQC-1 / Special Entry Darshan Complex), near ATC car parking.

Customary: Take holy bath in Swamy Pushkarini before midnight.

### Daily Quota for Angapradakshinam Tokens

  * Normal days (Sun-Thu): **750 free tokens**.
  * Saturdays: **500 tokens**.
  * Completely free — no payment for the seva.
  * Tokens released monthly in advance (e.g., March 2026 quota opened Dec 23, 2025 at 10:00 AM).

### How to Book Angapradakshinam Tickets (Online – Only Reliable Way Now)

TTD has stopped offline/same-day issuance at Tirumala. All tokens via **advance online booking** on official portal.

**Platform** : ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in (TTD website) or TTD mobile app.

**Booking Process** :

  1. Register/login with mobile number (OTP).
  2. Go to Sevas > Select Angapradakshinam.
  3. Choose date (system hides Fridays/festivals).
  4. Enter name, age, Aadhaar/Voter ID/Passport (photo ID mandatory).
  5. Max 2 persons per mobile number.
  6. Confirm — get e-ticket via email/SMS. Print it.
  7. Slots open at 10:00 AM on release date — be ready as they fill fast!

**Latest Updates (Feb 2026)** : Book 3-4 months ahead when quota releases (check TTD site or intirupati.in for dates). Some months used lucky dip earlier, but now mostly direct first-come-first-served.

### Angapradakshinam for Locals (Tirupati Urban/Rural & Tirumala)

A separate quota exists for locals via **e-Dip (electronic lucky draw)** — about **250 tokens** every Saturday.

**Process for Locals** :

  1. Register online on Thursday (10:00 AM – 4:00 PM) using Aadhaar.
  2. Lucky draw at 5:00 PM same day — results via SMS.
  3. If selected: Pay refundable ₹500 deposit online.
  4. Collect physical token next Friday (2:00 PM – 8:00 PM) at Mahathi Auditorium, Tirupati — show Aadhaar.
  5. Deposit refunded after ritual.

This is only for Tirupati/Tirumala residents — proof of address needed. Check TTD eDip portal for open dates.

### Angapradakshinam Tickets Offline / In Tirupati?

  * **Offline at Tirumala** : Stopped now — no same-day or current booking at PAC-1 or CRO.
  * **In Tirupati** : Only for locals via e-Dip (as above). No general offline for outsiders.
  * All devotees (including from Nellore) must use online advance booking.

### Important Rules & Entry Guidelines

  * **Eligibility** : No children below 12 years.
  * **Dress Code** : Traditional — Men: Dhoti (no shirt inside); Women: Saree/Half-saree/Churidar with dupatta. Wet clothes for rolling.
  * **What to Bring** : Printed e-ticket, original photo ID.
  * **Not Allowed** : Mobile phones, cameras, luggage, electronics.
  * **Entry Process** : Report at VQC-1 by 1:00 AM for verification. Perform ritual with sevaks’ help, then darshan.

### Tips for Nellore Devotees & Planning

  * Book early when quota opens — use fast internet.
  * Check official TTD site or app for exact release dates.
  * Combine with free Annaprasadam or other sevas.
  * For help: Visit intirupati.in or TTD helpline.

This profound seva brings immense blessings — plan with devotion! For latest booking alerts or Tirumala tips, follow intirupati.in.
