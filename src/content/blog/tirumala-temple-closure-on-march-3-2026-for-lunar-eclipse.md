In accordance with sacred traditions, the **Sri Venkateswara Temple in Tirumala will be closed** on **Tuesday, March 3, 2026** , due to the lunar eclipse (Chandra Grahanam).

The closure will be in effect from **9:00 AM to 7:30 PM** —approximately **10 hours and 30 minutes**.

The eclipse itself is scheduled from **3:20 PM to 6:47 PM**. As per scriptural guidelines, the temple doors will be shut **six hours before** the eclipse begins for purification purposes.

**Schedule for the Day**

  * **Temple Closes:** 9:00 AM
  * **Eclipse Period:** 3:20 PM to 6:47 PM
  * **Temple Reopens:** 7:30 PM (followed by special purification rituals)
  * **Public Darshan Resumes:** From **8:30 PM** onwards

**Cancellations of Sevas and Darshans**

All **Arjitha Sevas** (paid rituals) and several special darshan channels have been cancelled for the entire day of March 3rd.

Category| Cancelled Items  
---|---  
**Arjitha Sevas**|  Ashtadala Pada Padmaradhana, Kalyanotsavam, Unjal Seva, Arjitha Brahmotsavam, Sahasra Deepalankara Seva  
**Special Darshans**|  VIP/Protocol Darshan, Srivani Darshan, Rs. 300 Special Entry Darshan, Darshan for parents with infants, Senior Citizens & Differently-abled, Donor Darshan, NRI Darshan  
  
**Guidance for Devotees**

  * **Plan Accordingly:** If your pilgrimage coincides with this date, please adjust your travel plans. Expect **significant crowds** at the temple from 8:30 PM onward when darshan resumes.
  * **Check for Updates:** Always verify the latest information on the **official TTD website** or through their helpline (**155257**) closer to the date, as schedules are subject to change.

We hope this information helps you plan your pilgrimage smoothly. May your visit be filled with devotion.
