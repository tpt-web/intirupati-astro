> Your quick guide to securing a free darshan token at Tirupati’s key counter Live Status Info.

Planning your **Tirumala darshan** through the **Alipiri footpath**? The **Bhudevi Complex in Tirupati** is one of the main counters where you can obtain free **Sarva Darshan (SSD)** and **Divya Darshan tokens**. This guide provides clear, actionable information to help you navigate the process smoothly.

### 📍 Bhudevi Complex: Location & Key Details

The **Bhudevi Complex** is centrally located near the **Alipiri Bus Stand** in Tirupati, making it a convenient starting point for many pilgrims.

  * **Primary Function** : It is a **free token issuance counter** for devotees undertaking the darshan via the Alipiri footpath.
  * **Token Types Issued** : 
    * **Sarva Darshan (SSD) Tokens** : For general darshan.
    * **Divya Darshan Tokens** : Specifically for pilgrims who choose to walk the holy footpath to Tirumala.
  * **Cost** : All tokens issued here are **free of charge**.
  * **Process** : Tokens are issued on a **strict first-come, first-served basis** until the daily quota is exhausted. They are often issued for darshan on the **following day**.

### 🕒 Understanding Token Timings (With a Crucial Note)

**Please note: There are no fixed, permanent timings for token issuance at Bhudevi Complex.** The start times and daily quotas are set by the **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)** and can change daily based on crowd management and other factors.

However, based on common patterns observed, here is a general framework:

Token Type| Typical Issuance Start Time (Approximate)| What to Know  
---|---|---  
**Sarva Darshan (SSD) Tokens**|  Early Afternoon (e.g., 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)| Timing varies most. Tokens are usually for darshan slots the next day.  
**Divya Darshan (Footpath) Tokens**|  Late Afternoon (e.g., around 4:00 PM)| Issued for pilgrims commencing the walk. You may need to scan this token at **Srivari Mettu** to begin your trek.  
  
**🚨 Most Important Point:** The exact time token issuance begins is announced by TTD on a day-to-day basis. **Relying on yesterday’s or last week’s timings will likely lead to disappointment.**

### 🔍 How to Find the  _Live_ Token Status & Timings

To get accurate, real-time information, you must check the official sources on the day of your visit.

  1. **Official TTD Social Media (BEST METHOD)** : This is the most reliable source for live updates. The official **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams** accounts post about token quota status at Bhudevi and other counters. 
     * **Facebook** : `facebook.com/TTDevasthanams`
     * **Instagram & X (Twitter)**: Look for `@TTDevasthanam`
     * **Search for posts** with keywords like “SSD token,” “Bhudevi Complex,” or “quota completed.”
  2. **Visit the Counter in Person** : You can go to **Bhudevi Complex, Srinivasam, or Vishnu Nivasam** to check the current status. Be prepared for queues, especially on weekends and holidays.
  3. **Official TTD Website** : For general information and announcements, visit the official portal: **[www.tirumala.org](https://www.tirumala.org/)**.

### 📋 Important General Rules & Tips

  * **Carry ID Proof** : Always have a valid government photo ID (Aadhar, Voter ID, etc.) for all members of your group when collecting tokens.
  * **Be Early & Patient**: Queues form hours before the expected issuance time. The daily quota is limited and often finishes quickly.
  * **Other Counters** : If the quota at Bhudevi is full, check other **free darshan token counters** in Tirupati like **Srinivasam** and **Vishnu Nivasam**.
  * **Plan Your Stay** : Since tokens are often for the next day’s darshan, plan your **Tirupati accommodation** accordingly. You can find a complete guide to **TTD guest houses like Sri Padmavathi Guest House and Srinivasam Complex** on our website, **[intirupati.in](/)**.

### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1: What is the Bhudevi Complex Tirupati contact number?**  
A: TTD does not typically list a direct contact number for the token counter. For official inquiries, you can try the central **TTD Helpline** or rely on the live updates posted on their **official social media channels** , as mentioned above.

**Q2: Can I book Bhudevi Complex tokens online?**  
A: **No.** The SSD and Divya Darshan tokens issued at Bhudevi Complex are **free and cannot be booked online**. They must be obtained physically at the counter on a first-come, first-served basis. For other darshans that can be booked, see our **TTD Online Booking Guide** on **[intirupati.in](/)**.

**Q3: What does “Quota Completed” mean?**  
A: It means the **daily allocated limit of tokens for that specific counter (e.g., Bhudevi Complex) has been fully distributed for the day**. Issuance has stopped, and you will need to try another counter or return the next day.

**Q4: Are Bhudevi Complex token timings the same tomorrow?**  
A: **Not necessarily.** The start time for token issuance is decided daily by TTD based on various factors like crowd inflow and temple capacity. **Always check the live status on the day you plan to get a token.**

**Q5: What is the difference between SSD and Divya Darshan tokens at Bhudevi?**  
A: **SSD (Sarva Darshan) Tokens** are for the general darshan queue. **Divya Darshan Tokens** are specifically for pilgrims who wish to have darshan after walking the **Alipiri footpath** to Tirumala as an act of devotion.
