Dealing with persistent life challenges like marriage delays, infertility, chronic illnesses, financial struggles, family disputes, or psychological issues? These could signal **Pitru Dosha** (ancestral curse or unsatisfied Pitrus). **Pitru Dosha Pooja** (also called Pitra Dosh Nivaran Puja or Pitru Pooja) in Kashi (Varanasi) is one of the most powerful remedies. Known as the ultimate Dosha Nivarana Sthala, Kashi’s sacred Ganga ghats make rituals highly effective for appeasing ancestors, granting them peace in Pitru Loka, and removing doshas for descendants.

### What is Pitru Dosha Pooja in Kashi?

This Vedic ritual pacifies ancestors’ souls through offerings on the Ganga River ghats. It includes **Thila Homam** (to remove Pitru Shaap), **Pinda Daan** (rice ball offerings by a Brahmin), **Tarpan** , and sometimes **Moksha Narayan Bali** for unsatisfied souls (e.g., unnatural deaths). Performed by 3–4 experienced pandits, it lasts 3–4 hours. An elder family member (or woman via Sankalp) leads; Brahmin assists with key acts. Individual (not group) for best results. Kashi’s spiritual power (with Lord Vishnu as Pitrudevata) ensures profound effects.

### Benefits of Performing in Kashi

  * Complete removal of Pitru Dosha and ancestral curses
  * Relief from marriage/childbirth delays, long illnesses, financial blocks, and mental issues
  * Strengthened family bonds and harmony
  * Peace/moksha for departed souls
  * Overall prosperity, health, success, and protection from negative energies
  * Long-lasting blessings for the lineage

Best on Amavasya, Pitru Paksha, or Mahalaya Amavasya for maximum potency.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Duration** : 3–4 hours (morning slots preferred; fast during puja).
  * Performed **daily** on Ganga ghats.
  * Most auspicious: Amavasya days, Pitru Paksha (e.g., September–October 2026), Mahalaya Amavasya.
  * Report early after head bath; traditional attire mandatory. Dress code: Men — white dhoti + shirt/kurta; Women — saree/salwar kameez (no jeans/T-shirts).

### Location

Mainly on the sacred **Ganga River ghats** in Varanasi (Kashi), such as Dashashwamedh Ghat, Assi Ghat, or Manikarnika Ghat areas. Some include Pishach Mochan Kund for specific rituals like Narayan Bali.

### Important Rules and Tips

  * Abstain from non-veg/alcohol 3 days before/after.
  * Women: Perform after 5th day of cycle; can do Sankalp.
  * If recent family death: Wait 6 months.
  * Head bath before/after; discard puja clothes.
  * Feed Brahmins/cows post-ritual.
  * High demand during Pitru Paksha — plan stay near ghats.
  * Anyone can perform, even without confirmed Dosha.
