## **YSS Dhyana Mandir Inaugurated in Tirupati: A New Spiritual Landmark**

Tirupati, the spiritual capital of Andhra Pradesh, welcomes a new sanctuary for meditation and peace. The **Yogoda Satsanga Society (YSS) Dhyana Mandir** was inaugurated on **January 3** , marking a significant addition to the city’s sacred landscape. Located near the iconic Tirumala hills, this center is set to become a hub for devotees seeking inner calm through the teachings of Paramahansa Yogananda.

### **Key Highlights of the YSS Dhyana Mandir, Tirupati**

  * **Inauguration Date:** January 3, 2025
  * **Inaugurated by:** Swami Smaranananda Giri, representing YSS
  * **Purpose:** Meditation, spiritual discourses, and Yogananda’s Kriya Yoga teachings
  * **Significance:** A dedicated space for self-realization and community spiritual practice

This new center reinforces Tirupati’s status as a multi-denominational spiritual destination, where ancient temples and modern meditation centers coexist.

## **YSS Dhyana Mandir Tirupati: Location & Visitor Information**

For those planning a visit, here are the essential details:

  * **Address:** YSS Tirupati Center, Near Anna Raju Gardens, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.
