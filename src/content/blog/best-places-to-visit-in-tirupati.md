Tirupati, the spiritual hub of Andhra Pradesh, is world-famous for the **Sri Venkateswara Temple** in Tirumala, but the city and surroundings offer a mix of divine sites, nature, history, and wildlife. Whether you’re on a short pilgrimage from Nellore or planning a longer stay, here are the **best places to visit in Tirupati** (including Tirumala and nearby areas) for a fulfilling trip in 2026.

### 1\. [Sri Venkateswara Temple, Tirumala](/blog/list-of-ttd-temples-timings-open-closes-location/) (The Must-Visit)

The crown jewel — darshan of Lord Balaji. Plan for slotted Sarva Darshan or book Rs. 300 Special Entry in advance via ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. Combine with nearby spots like Papavinasanam Dam, Akasa Ganga, or Swami Pushkarini.

**Tip** : Reach early for shorter queues; tonsure (Kalyana Katta) and laddu prasadam are iconic.

### 2\. Sri [Padmavathi Ammavari](/blog/sri-padmavathi-ammavari-temple-tiruchanur/) Temple, Tiruchanur

Dedicated to Goddess Padmavathi (consort of Lord Venkateswara). Many devotees visit here first or last. Special Entry Rs. 200; book online. Serene atmosphere with beautiful lotus pond and Kumkum Archana for marital blessings.

**Distance** : ~5 km from Tirupati town.

### 3\. [Sri Govindaraja Swamy Temple](/blog/sri-govindaraja-swamy-temple-tirupati-history-timings-sevas-how-to-reach/)

Ancient temple in Tirupati town dedicated to Lord Vishnu (brother of Venkateswara). Features stunning architecture, daily sevas, and peaceful vibes. Free darshan; less crowded than Tirumala.

**Location** : Heart of Tirupati; easy walk from bus stand.

### 4\. [Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple (Kapila Theertham)](/blog/kapila-theertham-temple-sevas-timings-and-online-booking/)

One of the 108 sacred Shiva temples; waterfall (Kapila Theertham) flows directly into the shrine. Sacred bath in the falls is highly auspicious. Best early morning.

**Distance** : ~4–5 km from Tirupati town.

### 5\. Chandragiri Fort

Historic 11th-century fort with stunning views, sound-and-light show, and museum. Great for history lovers; evening shows are magical.

**Distance** : ~12–15 km from Tirupati; well-maintained by ASI.

### 6\. Sri Venkateswara Wildlife Sanctuary & Zoo

Home to deer, leopards, sloth bears, and birds. Enjoy safari, nature trails, and the zoo section. Ideal for families and nature enthusiasts.

**Distance** : ~10–15 km; entry fee nominal.

### 7\. Talakona Waterfalls

Highest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh (270 ft) inside Sri Venkateswara National Park. Trek, picnic, and enjoy the lush forest. Best post-monsoon (Oct–Feb).

**Distance** : ~60 km from Tirupati; hire cab or join tour.

### 8\. [Sri Kalahasti Temple](/blog/sri-kalahasti-temple-complete-guide-2026/)

Famous Shiva temple for Rahu-Ketu Pooja (Dosha Nivaran). Ancient architecture and powerful rituals. Combine with Tirupati visit.

**Distance** : ~38–40 km; frequent buses.

### 9\. Kanipakam Vinayaka Temple

Self-manifested (Swayambhu) Ganesh idol in a perennial spring. Famous for resolving disputes and wishes. Sweet prasadam (modak/laddu) is popular.

**Distance** : ~70 km; good day trip.

### 10\. Other Quick Mentions

  * **Sri Vari Museum** (Tirumala): Temple history and artifacts.
  * **Silathoranam** (Tirumala): Natural rock arch with geological significance.
  * **TTD Gardens & Deer Park** (Tirupati): Relaxing green spots.
  * **Sri Venkateswara Museum** (Tirupati): Rare idols and temple relics.

### How to Plan Your Trip from Nellore

  * **Distance** : ~130–140 km; 2.5–3 hours by train/bus/car.
  * **Best Time** : Oct–Mar (pleasant weather); avoid peak summer.
  * **Transport** : APSRTC buses (frequent), trains (Tirupati station), or private cab.
  * **Stay** : TTD rooms in Tirupati/Tirumala (book online); private hotels abundant.
  * **Tips** : Book temple darshan/sevas early; carry ID; traditional dress for temples; try local Andhra meals and laddus.

Tirupati blends spirituality, nature, and heritage perfectly — a trip that rejuvenates body and soul.

### FAQ

**Q: What are the top 3 must-visit places in Tirupati?** A: 1. Sri Venkateswara Temple (Tirumala), 2. Sri Padmavathi Temple (Tiruchanur), 3. Sri Kalahasti Temple.

**Q: How many days needed to cover best places?** A: 2–3 days (Day 1: Tirumala darshan; Day 2: Tiruchanur + town temples; Day 3: Nearby like Chandragiri or Kalahasti).

**Q: Are there any free attractions?** A: Yes — most temples have free darshan; wildlife sanctuary and falls have low entry fees.

**Q: Best time for waterfalls like Talakona?** A: Post-monsoon (Oct–Feb) when water flow is strong.

**Q: Where to get latest updates?** A: TTD official site (ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in) for temple info; AP Tourism or local sites for attractions.
