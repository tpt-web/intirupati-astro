Facing issues like marriage delays, career obstacles, health problems, financial instability, or Kaal Sarpa Dosha due to planetary afflictions? **Rahu Ketu Pooja** (also called Rahu Ketu Sarpa Dosha Nivaran or Kala Sarpa Dosha Parihara Pooja) at **Sri Kalahasti Temple** in Andhra Pradesh is one of the most powerful remedies. This ancient Shiva temple is renowned worldwide as the prime site for neutralizing Rahu and Ketu’s negative effects, as the idols of Rahu and Ketu are believed to have been pacified here by Lord Shiva.

### What is Rahu Ketu Pooja at Srikalahasti?

This Vedic ritual involves Sankalp (vow), offerings, mantra chanting, and homam to appease Rahu (north lunar node) and Ketu (south lunar node). It removes doshas from horoscopes, especially Kaal Sarpa Dosha (when all planets are between Rahu and Ketu). Performed in batches by temple priests, it lasts 30–45 minutes. Participants sit in designated mandapams; temple provides pooja materials (no need to buy extra from shops). Ideal for those with afflicted Rahu/Ketu in birth chart.

### Benefits of Rahu Ketu Pooja

  * Neutralizes Rahu/Ketu doshas and Kaal Sarpa effects
  * Removes obstacles in marriage, education, job, business, and progeny
  * Brings mental peace, health improvement, and financial stability
  * Protects from black magic, evil eyes, and negative energies
  * Grants success, courage, and overall prosperity Many devotees report life transformations after performing here.

### Ticket Cost and Details

Ticket prices vary by mandapam location and privileges (same ritual, different seating/proximity):

  * Rs. 500: Outside temple mandapam
  * Rs. 750: Outside temple mandapam
  * Rs. 1,500: Near Dwajasthambham (flagstaff)
  * Rs. 2,500: Inside temple mandapam
  * Rs. 5,000: Premium inside (e.g., near Sahasra Linga)
  * **Persons Allowed** : Usually 2 adults + 1 child per ticket (confirm at counter)
  * No additional materials needed; pooja items provided.
  * High demand during Rahu Kalam or auspicious days — arrive early.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Pooja Timings** : 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily (throughout the year)
  * **Temple Timings** : 6:00 AM – 9:00 PM (darshan up to 9:00 PM on some days)
  * Performed in batches; report 30–60 minutes early for your slot.
  * **Best Time** : During **Rahu Kalam** (daily varying; check Panchang) for maximum efficacy. Especially powerful on: 
    * Tuesdays and Sundays (Rahu/Ketu days)
    * Amavasya (new moon)
    * Pournami (full moon)
    * Grahan (eclipse) days
    * Nag Panchami, Rahu/Ketu Peyarchi
  * No specific “good day” restriction — any day works, but Rahu Kalam on Tuesdays/Sundays/Amavasya yields best results. Dress code: Traditional attire (men: dhoti/kurta; women: saree/salwar with dupatta). Head bath recommended; fast till pooja completion.

### Location

**Sri Kalahasti Temple** , Srikalahasti, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh (near Tirupati, about 40 km). Pooja happens in mandapams inside/around the temple premises.

### How to Book Rahu Ketu Pooja

**No official online booking** for Rahu Ketu Pooja as of 2026 — it’s counter-based only (same-day tickets, unlimited availability but first-come).

  1. Reach the temple early (especially for Rahu Kalam slots).
  2. Buy ticket at the designated counter inside premises (avoid outside agents charging extra).
  3. Provide basic details (names, gotra if needed); get pooja materials.
  4. Participate in the batch; focus on your wish during Sankalp.
  5. After pooja: Go straight home (avoid temples/other places that day); offer food to poor if possible.

Some third-party sites/agents offer “online” facilitation (e.g., via phone/WhatsApp), but official is in-person only. For general sevas/darshan, check aptemples.org or srikalahastitemple.org.in (limited online options).

### Important Rules and Tips

  * Pooja for same day only; no advance reservation.
  * After pooja: Avoid non-veg/alcohol; some fast the day.
  * High crowd on weekends/Amavasya — plan travel (bus/train from Nellore/Tirupati easy).
  * Temple near railway station; stay options available (book via TTD or private).
  * Combine with darshan or Rudrabhishekam for enhanced benefits.
  * For horoscope-specific advice, consult astrologer before.
