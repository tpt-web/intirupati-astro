## What is Srivani Trust (SRIVANI)?

**SRIVANI** stands for **Sri Venkateswara Aalayala Nirmanam Trust** – a special initiative by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) launched to construct, renovate, and maintain Hindu temples across India and worldwide.

### Purpose of Srivani Trust:

  * Building new Lord Venkateswara temples in remote areas
  * Restoring ancient Hindu temples
  * Promoting Dharma Prachara (religious propagation)
  * Supporting spiritual and cultural activities
  * Major project: Amaravati temple on Krishna River banks

**Key Highlight:** When you donate to Srivani Trust, you’re not just contributing to temple construction – you’re earning the privilege of VIP Break Darshan at Tirumala!

## How to Make Srivani Trust Donation Online (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Visit Official TTD Portal

**Website:** https://tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in

### Step 2: Register/Login

  * Click “Sign Up” or “Login”
  * Enter mobile number
  * Verify with OTP
  * Complete profile registration

### Step 3: Navigate to Donations Section

  * Go to **Menu → Donations**
  * Select **“Srivani Trust & E-Donations”**
  * Click **“Continue to Donate”**

### Step 4: Enter Donor Details

Field Required| Information to Provide| Important Notes  
---|---|---  
Full Name| As per Aadhaar/ID proof| Must match exactly  
Age| Actual age| Required for all donors  
Gender| Male/Female/Other| Mandatory field  
Email ID| Valid email address| For receipt & confirmation  
Mobile Number| Active mobile number| For OTP & updates  
Address| Complete postal address| For communication  
PAN Number| 10-digit PAN| **Mandatory for tax exemption**  
ID Proof Type| Aadhaar/Passport/DL| Select one  
ID Proof Number| Complete ID number| For verification  
  
### Step 5: Select Donation Amount

  * Choose donation slab (₹10,000 / ₹20,000 / custom amount)
  * For multiple people: Enter total amount (e.g., ₹30,000 for 3 persons)
  * Select number of pilgrims from dropdown

**Joint Donor Option:** If multiple people want tax benefits, add joint donor details separately

### Step 6: Payment Options

Payment Method| Processing Time| Transaction Charges  
---|---|---  
Credit Card| Instant| As per bank  
Debit Card| Instant| As per bank  
Net Banking| Instant| Varies by bank  
UPI| Instant| Minimal/None  
  
### Step 7: Download Documents

After successful payment:

  * **Donation Receipt** (for records)
  * **Tax Exemption Certificate (80G)** (for IT returns)
  * **Transaction Confirmation** (keep for darshan booking)

## FAQ – Srivani Trust Donations

**Q1. What’s the minimum donation amount?**  
₹10,000 per person for VIP Break Darshan eligibility.

**Q2. Can I donate less than ₹10,000?**  
Yes, but you won’t be eligible for VIP darshan. It will be a general donation.

**Q3. How many people can I book under one donation?**  
Maximum 9 persons per donation (₹1,00,000 limit). Each person needs ₹10,000 donation.

**Q4. Can my family share one ₹10,000 donation?**  
No. Each person needs individual ₹10,000 donation for separate darshan ticket.

**Q5. Is the donation refundable?**  
No. Donations are 100% non-refundable under all circumstances.

**Q6. What if I miss my darshan after donating?**  
No refund or rescheduling. You lose the darshan privilege.

**Q7. Can I donate today and book darshan tomorrow?**  
No. Darshan slots are released monthly. You must wait for release date.

**Q8. Is PAN card mandatory?**  
Yes, mandatory for getting 80G tax exemption certificate.

**Q9. Can NRIs and foreigners donate?**  
Yes, anyone can donate. NRIs use Passport/OCI as ID proof.

**Q10. How long is donation receipt valid?**  
Lifetime for tax purposes, but darshan eligibility expires after booking quota.

**Q11. Can I book VIP darshan for someone else?**  
No. Donor must be present for darshan. Tickets are non-transferable.

**Q12. What’s the difference between Srivani and ₹1 Lakh donor?**  
Srivani = one-time darshan. ₹1 Lakh donor = annual lifetime benefits.

**Q13. Do children need separate donation?**  
No. Children below 12 years can accompany parents for free.

**Q14. Can I use one donation for multiple darshan dates?**  
No. Each darshan booking consumes one ticket from your donation quota.

**Q15. What happens if counter is closed when I reach?**  
Quota exhausted for the day. Try another day or book online in advance.
