# 

> A beautiful float festival in Tirupati’s temple tank, featuring different deities each evening.

The Sri Govindaraja Swamy Temple in Tirupati will celebrate its **annual Teppotsavams (Float Festival)** for seven days, from **January 26 to February 01, 2026**. This is a beautiful event where the temple deities are taken on a ceremonial procession on floats in the sacred temple tank.

## Event Schedule & Daily Deities

Each evening of the festival features a different deity on the float, offering a unique darshan. The procession in the **Sri Govindaraja Swamy Pushkarini (temple tank)** happens daily from **6:30 PM to 8:00 PM**.

Here is the schedule of which deity will bless the devotees each day:

Date| Deity on the Float  
---|---  
**January 26**|  Sri Kodandarama Swamy  
**January 27**|  Sri Parthasarathy Swamy with Sri Rukmini and Satyabhama  
**January 28**|  Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy with Sridevi and Bhudevi  
**January 29**|  Sri Krishna Swamy with Andal Ammavaru  
**January 30, 31 & February 01**| Sri Govindaraja Swamy with His two divine Consorts  
  
## 🕉️ Festival Highlights

  * **Procession** : After the float ceremony in the temple tank, the deities will be taken in a procession through the temple streets to bless the devotees.
  * **Special Event** : On the final day (**February 01**), after the Teppotsavam, the deity will proceed to the shrine of **Sri Anjaneya Swamy** for a special darshan.
