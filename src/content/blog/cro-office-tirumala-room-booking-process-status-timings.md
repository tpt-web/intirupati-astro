The **Central Reception Office (CRO) in Tirumala** is the main hub for **on-the-spot accommodation allotment** for pilgrims arriving at the hill shrine. It handles general room bookings on a first-come, first-served basis.

### TTD CRO Office Timings, Location & Contact

Detail| Information  
---|---  
**Location**|  Opposite the **Tirumala Bus Station**.  
**Phone Number**|  The official phone number is **not listed in the available sources**. For inquiries, contacting the TTD’s 24×7 Call Center at **155257** is your best option.  
**Bus Stand to Office Distance**|  The office is located **directly opposite** the Tirumala Bus Station, so it is **very close** (a short walk).  
**General Booking Counter Timings**| **Opens at 6:00 AM** and **closes at 10:00 PM**.  
**Online Booking (ARP Counter) Timings**|  The **Advance Reservation Portal (ARP)** counter for pre-booked online accommodation is **open 24 hours**.  
  
### Room Booking: Process & Status

  * **Booking Method** : Rooms are allotted **on the spot** at the CRO office for **same-day stay only**. They are **not available for advance booking via phone or email**.
  * **Current Room Status** : Room availability is **dynamic** and depends on daily check-outs. The official TTD Facebook page (as seen in one link) sometimes posts **live updates on room availability**. For the most current status on the day of your visit, check the notice boards at the CRO office or follow official TTD social media.
  * **Online Booking** : For guaranteed accommodation, it is recommended to book **online in advance** through the official TTD seva portal (**ttdsevaonline**). If you have an online booking, you must collect your keys from the separate **ARP counter** , also located opposite the bus station.
  * **Required Documents** : A **photo ID proof** (like Aadhaar) is mandatory for all bookings. Please note that rooms are typically not allotted to single individuals or unmarried couples.
  * **Room Types & Cost**: The CRO office allots various TTD guest house rooms, with tariffs ranging from **₹50 to ₹1,000** per day.

### Key FAQs for Pilgrims

**Q1: What is the best time to queue at the CRO office for a room?**  
Queues start forming very early. To have a better chance, it is advisable to be at the office **well before the 6:00 AM opening time** , especially on weekends and holidays.

**Q2: How long does the room allotment process take?**  
The waiting time can vary from **2 to 4 hours or more** , depending on the crowd and availability. Pilgrims often wait in long queues outside the office.

**Q3: What if I have a donor pass or a recommendation letter?**  
The CRO office has separate cells for processing **Donor Passes** and **Recommendation Letters** from officials (MP, MLA, etc.). You should submit these documents at their respective counters for room allotment.

**Q4: Can I check room availability before traveling to Tirumala?**  
There is no live online tracker, but you can get a general idea by checking the official **TTD Facebook page** for periodic updates or by calling the **TTD helpline at 155257**.

**Q5: Are there other options if the CRO has no rooms?**  
If TTD rooms are unavailable, you can explore private lodges and cottages around Tirumala. Another option is to check with various **mutts (religious monasteries)** in the area, which sometimes offer basic accommodation to pilgrims.
