Are you dreaming of a divine wedding blessed by Lord Venkateswara? TTD Kalyana Vedika offers a beautiful, hassle-free option for Hindu couples to tie the knot in Tirumala at no cost. This scheme by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) has solemnized thousands of marriages, making it a popular choice for simple, sacred arranged unions under the Lord’s grace.

### What is TTD Kalyana Vedika?

TTD Kalyana Vedika is a special facility for conducting free Hindu marriages in Tirumala. It provides a dedicated hall (often at Purohit Sangam or Sri Vari Kalyana Mandapam) where couples can perform their wedding in batches based on auspicious Muhurtham timings. TTD supplies essentials like purohit services, pasupu-kumkum, kankanam, and blessings — all free of charge. The ceremony lasts about 30-40 minutes, and newlyweds receive prasadams plus free laddus.

This initiative promotes Hindu dharma, supports underprivileged couples, and keeps weddings simple yet spiritually fulfilling.

### Eligibility Criteria for Kalyana Vedika Marriage

To qualify, couples must meet these key requirements:

  * Both bride and groom must be Hindus.
  * Bride must be at least 18 years old; groom at least 21 years old.
  * Only arranged first marriages are allowed (no love marriages or second marriages).
  * Parents (or guardians with proper proof) must be present and give consent.
  * All details and proofs must match official documents.

### Required Documents for Registration

Prepare these originals and photocopies for verification:

  * Age proof for bride and groom (Birth Certificate, 10th/SSLC Marksheet, PAN Card, Passport, or Driving License).
  * Address proof (Aadhaar Card or Residential Certificate from MRO/Tahsildar).
  * Parents’ Aadhaar Cards (or Death Certificate if deceased; guardian proofs if applicable).
  * Unmarried certificate from MRO/Tahsildar (in some cases).
  * Passport-size photos of bride and groom.
  * Wedding invitation card (optional but helpful).

### How to Register Online for TTD Kalyana Vedika

The easiest way is through the official TTD portal:

  1. Visit the official TTD booking site: ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in (or check tirumala.org for updates).
  2. Register or log in with your details.
  3. Look for the E-Kalyana Vedika or Kalyana Mandapam section.
  4. Accept terms and conditions.
  5. Select your preferred Muhurtham date and time (availability depends on slots; advance booking up to 90 days possible in some cases).
  6. Fill in bride, groom, and parents’ details accurately.
  7. Upload required proofs (Aadhaar, age certificates, etc.).
  8. Submit to get a reservation/acknowledgment slip.

Reach Tirumala at least one day early or 6 hours before the Muhurtham for final verification at the office (near Purohit Sangam, Papavinasanam Road). Registration is free, but arrive prepared.

### Important Rules and Tips

  * Marriages happen in groups — arrive on time for your slot.
  * Free room allotment (one Rs.50 category room) may be available for one day; extras on payment.
  * After the wedding, enjoy Seegra Darshan (special darshan) for a nominal fee in some cases.
  * Contact TTD helpline (like 0877-2263433 or check official site) for latest availability.
  * Plan your Tirumala trip in advance due to crowd and travel rules.

### Why Choose Kalyana Vedika?

Getting married in Tirumala adds divine blessings and keeps costs minimal. Many couples share heartwarming stories of this sacred start to their life together.

### FAQ

**Q: Is the marriage completely free?** A: Yes, the hall, purohit, and basic items are free. Personal expenses like travel, clothes, or extra pooja items are on you.

**Q: Can we book for any date?** A: Slots depend on availability and Muhurtham. Book early online for better chances.

**Q: Are love marriages allowed?** A: No, only arranged marriages with parental presence and consent.

**Q: What if parents are not alive?** A: Guardians can attend with proper proofs like death certificates and relationship evidence.

**Q: Where is the Kalyana Vedika located?** A: Mainly at Purohit Sangam, Papavinasanam Road, near Vakula Matha Guesthouse, Tirumala.

For the most up-to-date info, always visit the official TTD website (ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in or tirumala.org). May Lord Venkateswara bless your union!
