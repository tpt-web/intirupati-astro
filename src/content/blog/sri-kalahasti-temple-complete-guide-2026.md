Sri Kalahasti Temple, revered as “Dakshina Kailasam” (Kailash of the South), stands as one of the most powerful and ancient Shiva temples in South India. Located in the spiritual town of Srikalahasti in Andhra Pradesh’s Tirupati district, this sacred shrine attracts millions of devotees annually who seek blessings for Rahu-Ketu dosha remedies, spiritual liberation, and divine grace.

## Sri Kalahasti Temple Rahu Ketu Pooja 2026

The **Rahu-Ketu Pooja** is the signature ritual of Sri Kalahasti Temple, performed to mitigate the malefic effects of Rahu and Ketu planets in one’s horoscope.

### What is Rahu Ketu Pooja?

Rahu and Ketu are shadow planets (nodes) in Vedic astrology. When they are negatively placed in a horoscope, they can cause:

  * Marriage delays and conflicts
  * Career obstacles
  * Health problems
  * Financial difficulties
  * Mental stress and anxiety
  * Education challenges

The Rahu-Ketu Pooja at Srikalahasti is considered the most powerful remedy for these doshas.

### Rahu Ketu Pooja Procedure

**Before the Pooja:**

  1. Purchase pooja ticket at designated counter
  2. Receive pooja materials (samagri) from counter
  3. Proceed to Rahu-Ketu Mandapam

**During the Pooja:**

  1. Devotees sit in rows on the floor
  2. Temple priests provide two silver idols (Rahu & Ketu)
  3. Follow priest’s instructions for ritual steps
  4. Perform abhishekam, archana, and offerings
  5. Recite mantras as guided by priest

**After the Pooja:**

  1. Receive prasadam and blessings
  2. The silver Rahu-Ketu idols can be taken home
  3. Continue daily worship at home as per guidance

### Information Required for Pooja

When booking Rahu-Ketu Pooja, provide:

  * **Name** (Full name of devotee)
  * **Nakshatra** (Birth star)
  * **Rashi** (Zodiac sign/Moon sign)
  * **Gotra** (Family lineage – optional)

_If you don’t know your nakshatra and rashi, consult family members or a local priest before visiting._

### Kalasarpa Dosha Nivarana Pooja

Sri Kalahasti Temple is particularly famous for **Kalasarpa Dosha Nivarana** , a specific type of Rahu-Ketu dosha. The same Rahu-Ketu Pooja serves as the remedy. Many devotees perform this pooja multiple times (once every 6 months) for severe cases.

## Sri Kalahasti Temple History & Significance

### Mythological Significance

The name “Srikalahasti” derives from three legendary devotees who attained salvation here:

  * **Sri** (Spider)
  * **Kala** (Serpent)
  * **Hasti** (Elephant)

Each worshipped Lord Shiva in their unique way with unwavering devotion and achieved moksha.

### Bhakta Kannappa Story

Sri Kalahasti is also famous for the story of **Kannappa** , a tribal hunter who offered his own eyes to Lord Shiva when he saw the deity’s eye bleeding. Moved by his supreme devotion, Lord Shiva stopped him and blessed him with liberation.

### Architectural Heritage

  * **Construction Period:** 5th century (inner temple), 12th century (outer temple)
  * **Dynasties:** Built and expanded by Chola and Vijayanagara kings
  * **Architecture Style:** Dravidian
  * **Special Feature:** Intricately carved stone pillars that remain fresh despite centuries
  * **Vayu Lingam:** The Shiva Lingam represents the Air element, one of the Pancha Bhoota Sthalams

### Pancha Bhoota Sthalam

Sri Kalahasti is one of five temples representing the five elements:

  1. **Srikalahasti** – Vayu (Air)
  2. **Tiruvannamalai** – Agni (Fire)
  3. **Kanchipuram** – Prithvi (Earth)
  4. **Chidambaram** – Akasha (Sky/Space)
  5. **Thiruvanaikaval** – Jal (Water)

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1\. What are the Sri Kalahasti Temple timings in 2026?

The temple is open daily from **5:30 AM to 9:00 PM** without any weekly closure. Rahu-Ketu Pooja is conducted from 6:30 AM to 8:30 PM with a lunch break from 1:30-2:30 PM.

### 2\. How can I book Srikalahasti Temple darshan tickets online?

Regular darshan tickets (Free, ₹50, ₹100) are **NOT available online** and must be purchased at temple counters. However, special sevas, poojas, and accommodation can be booked online through **[https://www.aptemples.org](https://www.aptemples.org/)**.

### 3\. What is the price of Rahu Ketu Pooja at Srikalahasti in 2026?

Rahu-Ketu Pooja ticket prices range from **₹150 (General) to ₹5,000 (Premium Couple)**. Mid-range tickets (₹500, ₹750, ₹1,500) are most popular.

### 4\. How do I book Rudrabhishekam at Srikalahasti Temple online?

Visit **[https://www.aptemples.org](https://www.aptemples.org/)** , select Srikalahasti Temple, navigate to “Sevas & Poojas,” choose Rudrabhishekam, select your preferred date/time, complete registration and payment. Prices range from ₹300 to ₹10,000+.

### 5\. Are there accommodation facilities at Sri Kalahasti Temple?

Yes, the temple Devasthanam provides guest rooms ranging from ₹100 (dormitory) to ₹1,500 (AC rooms). Book online through the AP Temples portal or contact temple office at +91-8578-222240.

### 6\. What is the contact number for Srikalahasti Temple?

**Main Numbers:** +91-8578-222240, +91-8578-222399 **Email:** [info@srikalahastitemple.org.in](mailto:info@srikalahastitemple.org.in)

### 7\. Is there a dress code for Srikalahasti Temple?

Yes. **Men** should wear dhoti, kurta-pajama, or shirt with trousers. **Women** should wear saree, salwar kameez, or traditional attire. Western casual wear like shorts and sleeveless tops are not allowed.

### 8\. What is special darshan in Srikalahasti Temple?

Special darshan options include ₹50 Quick Entry, ₹100 Special Darshan, and ₹500 Antharalaya Darshan (inner sanctum access). These provide faster access with reduced waiting times.

### 9\. What are the revised Antharalaya Darshan timings?

**Sunday:** 6:00 AM-3:00 PM only | **Monday & Saturday:** 1:00 PM-8:00 PM only | **Tuesday-Friday:** 6:00 AM-8:00 PM (full day)

### 10\. How far is Srikalahasti Temple from Tirupati?

Sri Kalahasti Temple is approximately **36-45 km** from Tirupati, taking about 1 hour by road. Frequent buses (every 15-30 minutes) are available from Tirupati bus stand.

### 11\. When is the best time to visit Sri Kalahasti Temple?

The best time is **October to March** (winter season) when weather is pleasant (15°C-28°C). For spiritual significance, visit during **Maha Shivaratri** (February), **Amavasya** (new moon), or **Mondays**.

### 12\. Can I perform Rahu Ketu Pooja without prior booking?

Yes, Rahu-Ketu Pooja tickets are available at temple counters on the same day without advance booking. However, during festivals and auspicious days, expect longer queues.

### 13\. What information is required for Rahu Ketu Pooja?

You need to provide: **Name** , **Nakshatra** (birth star), **Rashi** (zodiac sign), and optionally **Gotra** (family lineage). If unsure, consult family members or a priest beforehand.

### 14\. How long does Rahu Ketu Pooja take at Srikalahasti?

Each Rahu-Ketu Pooja session takes approximately **45 to 60 minutes**. The entire visit including waiting time typically takes 3-4 hours.

### 15\. Which days are most powerful for Rahu Ketu Pooja?

**Amavasya** (new moon), **Pournami** (full moon), **Sundays** , **Mondays** , **Eclipse days** , and during **Rahu Kalam** (specific 1.5-hour daily time slot) are considered most effective.

### 16\. Is online booking available for Srikalahasti Temple rooms?

Yes, Devasthanam rooms can be booked online through **[https://www.aptemples.org](https://www.aptemples.org/)** under the accommodation section. Book 15-30 days in advance during peak seasons.

### 17\. Does the temple remain open during solar and lunar eclipses?

Yes, Sri Kalahasti Temple uniquely **remains open during all eclipses** (solar and lunar). In fact, performing Rahu-Ketu Pooja during eclipse days is considered highly auspicious.

### 18\. Are there any hotels near Srikalahasti Temple?

Yes, numerous hotels are available within walking distance (1-15 minutes) from the temple, ranging from budget (₹500) to comfortable (₹4,000) options. Popular choices include Hotel Bluestone, Brigade Hotels, and The Lotus Park.

### 19\. How to reach Srikalahasti Temple from Tirupati?

**By Bus:** APSRTC buses every 15-30 minutes (₹40-₹60, 1 hour) **By Taxi:** ₹1,000-₹1,500 round trip **By Train:** Limited trains; bus is more convenient

### 20\. What is Kalasarpa Dosha and how does Srikalahasti help?

Kalasarpa Dosha occurs when all planets are hemmed between Rahu and Ketu in a horoscope. Srikalahasti Temple is the most famous place for **Kalasarpa Dosha Nivarana** through Rahu-Ketu Pooja, which devotees perform to mitigate negative effects.

### 21\. Can couples perform Rahu Ketu Pooja together?

Yes, couples can perform Rahu-Ketu Pooja together using **₹2,500 or ₹5,000 Couple tickets** , which provide better seating and a quieter environment.

### 22\. Is photography allowed inside Srikalahasti Temple?

Photography is allowed in **outer temple areas** with a camera fee of **₹100**. However, **mobile phones and cameras are strictly prohibited** inside the inner sanctum.

### 23\. What is the significance of Vayu Lingam at Srikalahasti?

The Shiva Lingam at Srikalahasti represents **Vayu** (Air element), one of the five Pancha Bhoota Sthalams. It’s believed that a lamp placed before the lingam flickers due to the natural air current, symbolizing Lord Shiva as Vayu.

### 24\. Are there any special poojas during Brahmotsavam?

Yes, during Maha Shivaratri Brahmotsavam (February 10-23, 2026), special poojas include **Nishita Kaal Puja** (most auspicious midnight ritual), continuous Rudrabhishekam, Rathothsavam (chariot procession), and various Abhishekam sevas.

### 25\. How can I check tomorrow’s timings at Srikalahasti Temple?

Visit **[https://srikalahastitemple.org.in](https://srikalahastitemple.org.in/)** for daily updates or call **+91-8578-222240**. Standard timings remain 5:30 AM-9:00 PM unless there’s a special festival or event.
