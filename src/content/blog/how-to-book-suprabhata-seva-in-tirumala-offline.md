Suprabhata Seva is a beautiful early morning ritual at Tirumala temple. It wakes up Lord Venkateswara with peaceful Vedic chants and songs. Devotees love this seva for the calm first darshan of the Lord.

**Suprabhata Seva Timings**

Suprabhata Seva happens daily from **3:00 AM to 4:00 AM** (sometimes listed as 2:30-3:00 AM in some schedules, but main ritual around 3 AM). It lasts about 30-60 minutes. **Gather at VQC-I** by **2:00 AM** if selected.

**Suprabhata Seva Cost**

The ticket price is **Rs. 120 per person**. It’s low-cost and includes the divine experience.

**Suprabhata Seva TTD How to Book / How to Book Suprabhata Seva** Booking is mainly through TTD’s lucky dip system (online or offline). Advance online lucky dip opens monthly on the official site ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. But many pilgrims ask about offline.

**How to Book Suprabhata Seva Offline / How to Get Suprabhata Seva Tickets Offline / How Can I Book Suprabhata Seva in Tirumala** If you’re already in Tirumala, try the **offline lucky dip** at CRO office (near bus station area). Only **50 tickets** per day for general public.

**Easy steps:**

  1. Go to **CRO office** in Tirumala.
  2. Register the day before (e.g., today for tomorrow’s seva).
  3. Enrollment time: **11 AM to 5 PM**.
  4. Bring photo ID (Aadhaar mandatory for Indians, passport for NRIs) – presence required.
  5. Take part in the lucky dip draw.
  6. Results announced at **6 PM** (check list or SMS).

If selected, report at **VQC-I** by **2:00 AM** with ID. Dress code: traditional clothes.

**Can We Book[Suprabhata Seva Online](/blog/suprabhatam-seva-at-tirumala-guide-to-book-your-tickets-now/)** Yes, mainly through TTD’s online lucky dip (electronic dip) on ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. It’s advance quota, opens monthly. Offline helps if you’re on spot and miss online.

**How to Apply for Suprabhata Seva** Apply via lucky dip (online on TTD site or offline at CRO). No direct paid booking – it’s luck-based due to high demand.

**Quick Tips**

  * Very limited spots, so try both ways.
  * Reach early, follow rules.
  * Many devotees get blessed this way!

Planning Tirumala trip? Try your luck for this holy Suprabhata Seva. Jai Venkateswara! May Lord Balaji bless you soon.
