**Srinivasam Complex** is TTD’s most popular and budget-friendly accommodation in Tirupati, located directly opposite the Tirupati Central Bus Stand. Offering rooms from just ₹200 to ₹600 per day, it’s the perfect choice for pilgrims seeking affordable, clean, and convenient stays with **100% online booking availability**.

**💡 Budget Tip:** The Non-AC rooms at ₹200 offer excellent value, especially during winter months (November-February). The AC rooms at ₹400-₹600 are worth considering during hot summer months (March-June). These are official TTD rates with no hidden charges! 

## 📱 How to Book Srinivasam Complex Online

Srinivasam Complex accepts **100% online bookings** through the official TTD website. Here’s your complete booking guide:

1

#### Create TTD Account

Visit **ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in** and create your account with email, mobile number, and upload a photo (mandatory).

2

#### Select Accommodation

Navigate to “Accommodation” section and choose “Srinivasam Complex” from available options.

3

#### Choose Dates & Room Type

Select your check-in date (up to 90 days advance) and preferred room category (Non-AC ₹200, AC ₹400, AC Deluxe ₹600).

4

#### Complete Payment

Make secure online payment using credit/debit card, net banking, or UPI. Save your booking confirmation.

5

#### Check-in Process

Arrive at Srinivasam Complex with printed booking receipt and original ID proof for check-in.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT BOOKING RULES:**

  * **Online Booking Only:** No walk-in or current booking facility
  * **90-Day Advance:** Book up to 90 days before check-in
  * **24-Hour Stay:** Rooms allotted for exactly 24 hours from check-in
  * **Cancellation Policy:** Base tariff refunded (GST not refundable)
  * **ID Mandatory:** Original ID proof required during check-in
  * **No Room Transfer:** Booking cannot be transferred to another person

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I get walk-in booking at Srinivasam Complex?**

**No.** Srinivasam Complex accepts **100% online bookings only** through ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. No current booking or walk-in facilities are available.

**Q: What is the cheapest room available?**

The **Non A/C Rooms** at **₹200 per day** are the most affordable option. They are basic but clean with attached bathrooms.

**Q: How early should I book online?**

Book **exactly 90 days in advance** when quota opens. Weekend and festival dates get booked within hours of opening.

**Q: Is parking available?**

**Yes.** Ample parking space for both cars and two-wheelers is available within the complex premises.

**Q: Can I cancel my booking?**

Yes, but only the **base tariff** will be refunded. The GST component is **not refundable**.

**Q: What facilities are shared with Madhavam?**

Srinivasam guests can access Madhavam’s AC facilities, while Madhavam guests can use Srinivasam’s banking, medical, and shopping facilities.

**Q: Are there food options nearby?**

**Yes.** Multiple vegetarian restaurants and eateries are within 5-minute walking distance from the complex.
