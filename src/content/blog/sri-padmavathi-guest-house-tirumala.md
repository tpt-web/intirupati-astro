## 📱 How to Book

  1. Visit ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in
  2. Select “Accommodation at Tirumala”
  3. Choose Sri Padmavathi Guest House
  4. Select dates and room type
  5. Complete online payment

[Book Online Now →](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/)

**💡 Pro Tip:** Book 90 days in advance for best availability. Weekday bookings have better chances than weekends.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the cheapest room at Sri Padmavathi Guest House?

The Ordinary Room at ₹600 per day is the most affordable option. It accommodates 2 persons and includes basic amenities.

Q2: How far is Sri Padmavathi Guest House from the main temple?

It’s approximately 10-15 minutes walking distance to the Tirumala Temple. The location is considered central and convenient.

Q3: Can I book Sri Padmavathi Guest House offline?

No, it’s online booking only through ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. No walk-in or counter booking is available.

Q4: What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is at 12:00 PM and check-out is at 10:00 AM. The room is allotted for 22 hours from check-in time.

Q5: Are there any age restrictions for booking?

No age restrictions, but minors must be accompanied by adults. Couples must provide valid marriage proof during check-in.

Q6: Is food available at Sri Padmavathi Guest House?

No in-house restaurant, but Annadanam counters are nearby. You can also bring outside food to your room.

Q7: Can I extend my stay after check-in?

Extensions are subject to availability. You need to request at the reception desk and pay additional charges.

Q8: What documents are required for check-in?

Original ID proof (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID), booking confirmation printout, and marriage proof for couples.

Q9: Is parking available for guests?

Limited parking is available on first-come-first-serve basis. It’s recommended to use TTD’s designated parking areas.

Q10: Can I cancel my booking and get a refund?

Yes, but only the base tariff is refunded. GST charges are non-refundable. Cancel through your TTD account online.

Q11: Are there any facilities for disabled pilgrims?

Basic accessibility features are available. Request ground floor rooms during booking for easier access.

Q12: What’s the best time to book for weekends?

Book exactly 90 days in advance at 12:00 AM when quota opens. Weekend bookings fill within minutes.
