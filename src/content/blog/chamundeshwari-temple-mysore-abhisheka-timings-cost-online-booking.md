Seeking the divine blessings of Goddess Chamundeshwari (an incarnation of Goddess Durga) through a sacred ritual bath? **Abhisheka** (also called Abhishekam or Rudrabhishekam variants) at Chamundeshwari Temple on Chamundi Hill, Mysore (Mysuru), is a powerful seva where the idol is bathed with sacred items like milk, honey, ghee, Panchamrutha, or water while chanting mantras. This brings protection, victory over obstacles, health, prosperity, and removal of negative energies. The temple, atop Chamundi Hill (about 1,000 steps or road access from Mysuru city), is a major Shakti Peetham and one of Mysore’s most revered sites.

### What is Chamundeshwari Abhisheka?

Abhisheka is performed daily in the early morning and evening slots. Types include:

  * **Panchamrutha Abhisheka** : Bath with five nectars (milk, curd, ghee, honey, sugar).
  * **Ekavara Abhisheka** : Single-time ritual (often Rudra chanting).
  * **Ekadashavara Abhisheka** : 11-time ritual (more elaborate, for stronger blessings). The ritual lasts 30–45 minutes, with temple providing pooja materials. Participants can witness or sponsor; prasadam (blessed items) available post-abhisheka.

### Benefits of Performing Abhisheka

  * Removal of doshas, enemies, and life hurdles
  * Good health, wealth, success in endeavors
  * Protection and courage (as Goddess Chamundeshwari slayed demons)
  * Family harmony and spiritual upliftment
  * Special grace on Fridays (auspicious for Devi)

Ideal during Navratri, Fridays, or personal muhurats.

### Ticket Cost and Types

From official and reliable sources (2026 updates):

  * **Panchamrutha Abhisheka** : Rs. 220 per person
  * **Ekavara Abhisheka** : Rs. 300 per person
  * **Ekadashavara Abhisheka** : Rs. 550 for 2 persons (or similar; confirm on portal) Other sevas may vary; prasadam distributed post-ritual (morning 7:30 AM–10:30 AM, evening 7:30 PM–9:00 PM). No entry fee for temple; special/VIP darshan Rs. 30–100 separately.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Abhisheka Slots** : 
    * Morning: 6:00 AM – 7:30 AM (daily)
    * Evening: 6:00 PM – 7:30 PM (daily)
    * Friday Special: 5:00 AM – 6:30 AM (earlier morning slot)
  * **Temple Darshan/Pooja Timings** : 7:30 AM – 2:00 PM; 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM; 7:30 PM – 9:00 PM
  * Performed daily; high demand on Fridays, festivals (e.g., Navratri, Dasara). Report early after head bath; traditional attire required (men: dhoti/kurta; women: saree/salwar with dupatta; no jeans/T-shirts).

### Location

**Sri Chamundeshwari Temple** , Chamundi Hill, Mysuru, Karnataka – 570010. About 13 km from Mysuru city center; reachable by KSRTC bus (every 20 mins), auto, or car (hill road). Steps climb available for devotees.

### How to Book Abhisheka/Seva Online in 2026

Booking is fully online via the official portal — limited slots, book early!

  1. Visit the official site: **chamundeshwaritemple.in** → E-Seva Booking (or check chamundeshwaritemple.in/Bookticket.aspx). 
     * Alternative: Some sevas via **itms.kar.nic.in/hrcehome** (Karnataka HRCE portal).
  2. Register/login with details (mobile/email).
  3. Select seva (e.g., Abhisheka type), date, and slot.
  4. Enter participant details (names, count).
  5. Pay online and download/print ticket (mandatory to show at temple).
  6. Arrive with printed ticket and ID for verification.

Advance booking available (current month or limited ahead); check availability daily. For queries: Temple office +91-0821-2590027 or [eochamunditemple@gmail.com](mailto:eochamunditemple@gmail.com).
