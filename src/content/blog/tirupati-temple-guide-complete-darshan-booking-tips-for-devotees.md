Planning a pilgrimage to the sacred **Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple** (Tirupati Balaji) at Tirumala? This world-famous Vaishnavite temple, managed by **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)** , attracts millions seeking Lord Venkateswara’s blessings for prosperity, health, and obstacle removal. As of February 2026, here’s a practical, up-to-date guide to make your visit smooth and divine.

### Why Visit Tirupati Temple?

Known as “Bhuloka Vaikuntam” (Heaven on Earth), the temple is one of the richest and most visited in the world. Devotees believe darshan here fulfills wishes and grants moksha. Key highlights: The self-manifested idol of Lord Balaji, daily rituals like Suprabhatam, and famous laddus as prasadam.

### Types of Darshan Available

  * **Sarva Darshan (Free)** : Open to all; waiting time 5–12+ hours (slotted system in 2026).
  * **[Special Entry Darshan (SED – Rs. 300)](/blog/tirumala-free-darshan-ssd-token-guide-live-crowd-status-waiting-times/)** : Faster queue (2–4 hours); advance online booking monthly.
  * **[VIP Break Darshan](/blog/how-to-book-srivani-darshan-tickets-online-offline/) / [Srivani Trust](/blog/how-to-book-vip-darshan-ttd-srivani-trust-donations-e-donations-slabs-benefits-details/)**: Rs. 10,000 donation + Rs. 500 ticket for priority access.
  * **Divya Darshan** : For pedestrians climbing steps (free, token-based).
  * **[Special for Seniors/Disabled](/blog/free-darshan-for-senior-citizens/)** : Priority queue with assistance.
  * **[Arjitha Sevas](/blog/arjitha-sevas-types-booking-reporting-timing-rules-dress-code/)** : Paid rituals like [Suprabhatam](/blog/how-to-book-suprabhata-seva-in-tirumala-offline/), Thomala Seva (advance lucky dip/online).

Book only via official TTD portal — avoid third parties!

### Darshan Timings (Daily Schedule )

Temple opens early; darshan runs almost 24 hours with breaks for sevas:

  * Suprabhatam: 2:30–3:00 AM
  * General Darshan: From ~3:00 AM onward (peaks 9:30 AM–7:00 PM)
  * Night Darshan: Up to ~12:30 AM (Ekanta Seva at 00:45 AM)
  * Best slots: Early morning or late night for shorter waits. Check live status on ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in for slotted Sarva Darshan.

### How to Book Darshan/Sevas/Accommodation Online

  1. Visit official portal: **ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in** (or TTD app).
  2. Register/login with mobile/Aadhaar.
  3. Choose Darshan/Seva/Accommodation.
  4. Select date/slot (advance quotas release monthly, e.g., May 2026 in late Feb).
  5. Enter details (up to 6 persons; ID proof required).
  6. Pay and download e-ticket (print mandatory).

  * **SED Rs. 300** : Monthly quota (e.g., May 2026 ~Feb 24 at 10 AM).
  * **Arjitha Sevas** : Lucky dip or direct (limited per user: 1 seva in 180 days).
  * **Accommodation** : Tirumala rooms (Rs. 50–few thousand); book advance.
  * **Current Booking** : Some same-day options (e.g., Break Darshan at 9 AM–2 PM).

Always verify on official site — quotas sell out fast!

### How to Reach Tirumala

  * **From Tirupati** : 20–25 km uphill; APSRTC buses (every 10–15 mins, ~1 hour), private taxis, or walk (steps ~9 km, Divya Darshan token required).
  * **Air** : Nearest airport Tirupati (TIR) or Chennai (MAA, ~130 km).
  * **Train** : Tirupati Railway Station (major connectivity).
  * **Road** : Well-connected highways; parking at foothill.

### Accommodation & Facilities

  * TTD provides free/paid rooms (book online); private hotels in Tirupati.
  * Free meals (Annadanam) at temples.
  * Medical help, cloak rooms, and info centers available.

### Important Rules & Tips

  * **Dress Code** : Traditional only — Men: Dhoti/kurta/pyjama with upper cloth; Women: Saree/salwar with dupatta (no jeans, shorts, T-shirts, modern wear).
  * **Hair Tonsure (Kalyana Katta)** : Optional but auspicious (symbolic ego surrender); free at multiple halls in Tirumala (do before darshan; bath after).
  * **Prasadam** : Famous Tirupati Laddu (Rs. 50–100 per piece; book online or counter); collect in Tirumala only.
  * **Prohibitions** : No mobiles/cameras inside sanctum; no non-veg/alcohol/smoking in Tirumala; no footwear in temple; bathe before entry; avoid menstruation for women in some sevas.
  * **Other Tips** : Carry ID proof; book early; plan for crowds (peak in festivals like Brahmotsavam); use free buses in Tirumala; head bath after tonsure.

### FAQ

**Q: How to book Rs. 300 Special Entry Darshan in 2026?** A: Online via ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in; monthly quotas release ~2–3 months ahead (e.g., May 2026 in late Feb at 10 AM).

**Q: Is tonsure mandatory?** A: No, but highly recommended and free; do it first, then bathe and darshan.

**Q: Can children/seniors get priority?** A: Yes, special queues; infants free; seniors/disabled have assistance.

**Q: What is the best time to visit?** A: Sept–Feb (pleasant weather); avoid peak festivals for shorter waits.

**Q: Where to get latest updates?** A: Official TTD sites (ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in or tirumala.org); avoid unofficial sources.
