## Sort by Courier vs. Sort by SKU — When to Use Which?

*   **Sort by Courier** is best when you dispatch through multiple courier partners (like Delhivery, Valmo, Ecom Express, XpressBees). Gaping labels by courier partner allows your team to hand over shipments in organized lots, reducing missing scan issues.
*   **Sort by SKU** is ideal for warehouse picking. When all labels for the same product are grouped together, you can pick the required inventory in one go and pack them in assembly-line style, dramatically reducing shipping errors.
