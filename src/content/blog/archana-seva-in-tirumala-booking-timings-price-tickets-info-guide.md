Visiting **Tirumala** is a dream for many devotees. Among all the temple services, **Archana Seva** holds a very special place. It is calm, divine, and allows devotees to feel spiritually connected to Lord Venkateswara.

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### **What is Archana Seva?**

Archana Seva is a sacred ritual performed early in the morning at Tirumala temple. During this seva, temple priests chant the **holy names of Lord Venkateswara** with devotion. The chanting is done slowly and peacefully, creating a spiritual atmosphere.

The seva is performed **in the devotee’s name** , asking the Lord for blessings such as good health, peace of mind, and family happiness.

### **Why Archana Seva is Special**

  * Calm and peaceful early-morning darshan
  * Less crowd compared to general darshan
  * Devotees stand close to the sanctum
  * Holy chanting creates positive energy
  * Ideal for spiritual seekers and elders

Many devotees feel deep inner peace after attending Archana Seva.

### **Archana Seva Timings**

  * **Reporting Time:** 4:00 AM
  * **Seva Starts:** 4:30 AM
  * **Duration:** About 30 minutes
  * **Seva Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
  * **Ticket Cost:** Rs. 220 per person
  * **Prasadam:** One laddu per ticket

Timings may change during festival or peak seasons.

### **Dress Code You Must Follow**

Traditional dress is compulsory for this seva.

**For Men**

  * White dhoti or pancha
  * Shirt and vest must be removed during seva

**For Women**

  * Saree with blouse
  * Or salwar kameez with dupatta

Devotees not following dress rules may not be allowed inside.

### **How to Book Archana Seva Tickets**

**Online Method**

  * Tickets are released in advance
  * Selection is done through a lucky draw
  * If selected, payment must be completed on time

**Offline Method**

  * Limited tickets are available at Tirumala
  * Registration is done one day before
  * Selected devotees can collect tickets

Due to high demand, booking early is always recommended.

### **Rules for Children**

  * Children below 12 years can attend free with age proof
  * Children below 3 years are usually not allowed
  * Children must follow dress and discipline rules

### **Helpful Tips for Devotees**

  * Reach the reporting point early
  * Carry original ID proof
  * Follow temple instructions calmly
  * Avoid agents or middlemen
  * Maintain silence and devotion

### **Final Thoughts**

Archana Seva is not just a ritual; it is a peaceful spiritual experience. If you want to feel closer to Lord Venkateswara and enjoy a calm darshan, Archana Seva is a beautiful choice.

**FAQ**

### **What is Archana Seva in Tirumala?**

Archana Seva is a special early-morning ritual where priests chant the holy names of Lord Venkateswara. The seva is performed in the devotee’s name to seek blessings for peace, health, and prosperity.

### **What is the timing of Archana Seva?**

Archana Seva reporting time is 4:00 AM and the seva starts at 4:30 AM. It usually lasts for about 30 minutes and is performed on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.

### **How much is the Archana Seva ticket cost?**

The Archana Seva ticket costs Rs. 220 per person. Devotees receive one laddu as prasadam after completing the seva.

### **How can I book Archana Seva tickets?**

Archana Seva tickets can be booked online through a lucky dip system or offline through limited lucky dip registration at Tirumala, depending on availability.

### **Is dress code compulsory for Archana Seva?**

Yes, traditional dress is mandatory. Men must wear dhoti or pancha, and women should wear saree or salwar kameez with dupatta to attend Archana Seva.
