Facing delays in marriage or seeking blessings for a happy marital life? **Sree Thirumandhamkunnu Bhagavathy Temple** (also called Thirumandhamkunnu Bhagavathy Kshethram or Thirumandhamkunnu Devi Temple) in Angadippuram, Malappuram district, Kerala, is a renowned ancient Mahakshethram dedicated to Goddess Bhadrakali (Bhagavathy). This hilltop shrine is especially famous for **Mangalya Pooja** (offered to Unni Ganapathy/Unni Ganapathi) to remove obstacles to marriage and ensure matrimonial bliss. As one of Kerala’s foremost Bhadrakali temples, it attracts thousands for this votive offering, along with its scenic beauty, peaceful ambiance, and powerful rituals.

### What is Thirumandhamkunnu Bhagavathy Temple?

Located atop a hillock in Angadippuram (near Perinthalmanna), the temple is managed by **Sree Thirumandhamkunnu Bhagavathy Devaswom**. It features the goddess as the tutelary deity of Valluvanad, with shrines for Ganapathy, Shiva, and others. The temple’s history ties to King Mandhatha of the Surya dynasty, who meditated here. It’s celebrated for its 10-day annual festival (Pooram) and Mangalya Pooja, believed to grant quick alliances and marital harmony.

### Benefits of Mangalya Pooja

  * Removes hurdles to marriage and ensures timely, happy unions
  * Blessings for unmarried girls/boys to find suitable partners
  * Protection from doshas affecting family life
  * Overall prosperity, peace, and fulfillment in relationships Three consecutive years’ poojas are traditionally recommended (even if marriage happens earlier) for full efficacy.

### Mangalya Pooja Details

  * **Days Performed** : Fridays, Sundays, and Tuesdays (expanded from only first Friday of Thulam Malayalam month to handle crowds).
  * **Timings** : Usually after 9:30 AM (Pantheeradi Pooja time); pooja happens between 9:00–11:00 AM or as per schedule. Arrive early to queue vessels for prasadam collection (appam, payasam, ada).
  * **Rates (Vazhipadu)** : 
    * First Year: Rs. 700
    * Second Year: Rs. 1200
    * Third Year: Rs. 1700 (Rates from official Vazhipadu list; slight variations possible — confirm on site or contact).
  * **Prasadam** : Collect after pooja; bring vessels for distribution.

### Temple Darshan & Pooja Timings

  * **Morning** : Opens ~5:00–6:00 AM; Usha Pooja ~6:00 AM; Pantheeradi Pooja 9:30 AM; Ucha Pooja 11:30 AM.
  * **Afternoon Break** : ~12:00 PM – 4:00–4:30 PM.
  * **Evening** : Opens ~4:00–4:30 PM; Thirinju Pantheeradi Pooja 4:30 PM; Athazha Pooja ~8:00 PM.
  * **Closing** : ~8:00–8:30 PM. Dress code: Traditional Kerala attire (men bare-chested in sanctum; women saree/salwar). Head bath recommended.

### How to Book Mangalya Pooja or Other Vazhipadu

  * **Online Booking** : Available via official site (thirumandhamkunnutemple.com/vazhipadu_booking.php) for general Vazhipadu (fill form, pay, get prasadam mailed). Some sources mention WhatsApp-powered booking at booking.thirumandhamkunnutemple.com.
  * **For Mangalya Pooja** : Advance booking advised (up to 3 months wait possible due to demand); can book online or directly at temple counter. No strict “walk-in only” — online form helps reserve date.
  * **Contact** : Manager, Sree Thirumandhamkunnu Bhagavathy Devaswom, Post Box No.5, Angadippuram – 679321, Malappuram Dist., Kerala. Phone: +91-4933-258820, +91-4933-258555. Fax: +91-4933-253820.
  * **Other Poojas** : Online for Vazhipadu like Valiya Vilakku, Maha Ganapathi Homam; prasadam courier available.

### Room Booking & Accommodation

Temple may have limited guest facilities (inquire via phone). Nearby options: Private homestays/hotels in Perinthalmanna/Angadippuram (e.g., Narmada Homestay near temple). No specific temple room prices listed — contact devaswom for details or book private stays (Rs. 800+).

### How to Reach

  * **Location** : Angadippuram, Perinthalmanna, Malappuram district, Kerala (on NH-966, Palakkad-Kozhikode route).
  * **By Road** : ~4 km from Perinthalmanna; well-connected buses/taxis.
  * **By Train** : Angadippuram Railway Station (~1.5 km).
  * **By Air** : Nearest Calicut International Airport (CCJ ~50–60 km).

### Important Rules & Tips

  * Arrive early for Mangalya Pooja (queue vessels in shed for prasadam).
  * High crowd on Fridays/Sundays/Tuesdays — book advance.
  * Carry ID; no mobiles/cameras in sanctum.
  * Combine with temple festival (annual Pooram ~10 days, vibrant).
  * Prasadam delicious — collect promptly.
