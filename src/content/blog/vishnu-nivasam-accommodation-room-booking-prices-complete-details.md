## 🚶 Current Booking (Walk-in Without Advance Booking)

Don’t have online booking? No problem! 50% of Vishnu Nivasam rooms are still available for current booking on arrival.

### Walk-in Booking Process:

#### Steps for Current Booking:

  1. Arrive at Vishnu Nivasam (opposite railway station)
  2. Go to the room allotment counter on ground floor
  3. Show valid ID proof (Aadhaar/Driving License/Voter ID/Passport)
  4. Pay caution deposit + room charges
  5. Receive room keys and check in immediately

**✅ Pro Tip for Walk-ins:** Try to reach before 11 AM or after 3 PM when crowds are lower. Tuesday-Thursday are the best days for getting rooms without much wait. Weekend arrivals should reach by 8-9 AM as rooms fill up quickly! 

## ⏰ Room Extension & Checkout Rules

#### Standard Stay Duration

  * 24 hours from check-in time (not calendar day)
  * For example: Check-in at 3 PM = Checkout by 3 PM next day
  * Checkout must be done by the person who booked

#### Want to Extend Your Stay?

  * **1-Day Extension:** Visit ground floor counter, pay same rate, inform reception in advance
  * **2+ Days Extension:** Double rent charged (to prevent misuse of accommodation)
  * **Important:** Extension subject to availability only

**🚫 What’s NOT Allowed:**

  * Room cancellations – no refunds on confirmed bookings
  * Changing booking to another person’s name
  * Transferring your room to someone else
  * Future date bookings during walk-in (only same-day current booking)

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is online booking really available for Vishnu Nivasam?**

Yes! As per official TTD website, 50% of rooms can be booked online 60 days in advance at ttdsevaonline.com. The remaining 50% are for current booking (walk-in).

**Q: Can bachelors/single men get rooms?**

Unrelated male groups may face restrictions. However, brothers, father-son, or related family members can get rooms. Couples must show marriage proof.

**Q: Is car parking available?**

Yes! Excellent parking facilities for both cars and two-wheelers. Very spacious and secure.

**Q: What if I couldn’t book online and rooms are full when I arrive?**

Try Srinivasam Complex (5 min walk), Madhavam Guest House, or private hotels near bus stand. You can also call TTD helpline 0877-2233333 for other options.

**Q: How early should I book online?**

Book exactly 60 days before your travel date when quota opens. Weekend/festival bookings get sold out within hours, so set a reminder!

**Q: Is food available?**

Yes! Ground floor has a restaurant serving all meals at reasonable rates. Outside food is also allowed in rooms.

**Q: Can I book for future dates during walk-in?**

No. Current booking (walk-in) is only for same-day check-in. For advance booking, use the online portal.
