Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple, more popularly known as Kapila Theertham Temple, is a significant ancient shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva in Tirupati. Located at the foothills of the Tirumala range, it is renowned for the sacred waterfall (theertham) within its premises. This guide provides all essential details about temple timings, daily rituals, special sevas, and online booking for a well-planned pilgrimage.

## Temple Timings and Darshan Schedule

The Kapila Theertham Temple is open every day from **5:00 AM to 9:00 PM**.

Darshan Type| Daily Timings  
---|---  
**General Sarva Darshan**|  8:00 AM – 11:00 AM, 11:30 AM – 4:00 PM, 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM  
**Temple Opening Hours**|  5:00 AM – 9:00 PM  
  
## Daily Sevas and Rituals Schedule

The temple follows a strict schedule of rituals (sevas) throughout the day. Witnessing these ceremonies is a profound spiritual experience.

Sarva Darshan| 7:00 AM – 4:00 PM

Sahasra Namarchana| 4:30 PM – 5:00 PM  
Lalitha Sahasra Namarchana| 5:00 PM – 5:30 PM  
Deeparadhana| 6:00 PM – 6:30 PM  
Sarva Darshan (Evening)| 5:30 PM – 8:00 PM  
Ekantha Seva| 8:00 PM – 8:15 PM

## Online Booking and Important Information

**Seva Online Booking** : A few of the sevas at Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple can be booked online through the official temple or TTD (Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams) portals. Devotees are advised to check the official website for the most current booking links, availability, and updated tariffs.

**General Guidelines** :

  * The best time for a peaceful darshan is during the early morning hours.
  * Dress code is modest and conservative as per traditional temple customs.
  * The sacred waterfall (“theertham”) is a major highlight; visitors can often take a holy dip or collect the water.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What are the general darshan timings at Kapila Theertham Temple?**  
A: General Sarva Darshan is available in three slots: 8-11 AM, 11:30 AM-4 PM, and 6:30-8 PM.

**Q: Can I book sevas online for this temple?**  
A: Yes, select sevas can be booked online via the official TTD or temple booking website.

**Q: Is there a special seva on Mondays?**  
A: Yes, Unjal Seva is performed every Monday from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM.

**Q: What is the most important monthly festival at the temple?**  
A: Masa Sivaratri (monthly Shivaratri) is a key observance, featuring special sevas like Mahanyasa Abhishekam and Kalyanotsavam.
