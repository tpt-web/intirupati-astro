Suprabhata Seva (also called Suprabhatam) is the most peaceful early morning ritual at Tirumala temple. Priests wake Lord Venkateswara with sweet Vedic chants like “Kausalya Supraja Rama”. Many devotees want this close darshan – it’s divine and calm!

## Why is Suprabhatam Seva Special?

Getting Suprabhatam tickets means you’ll experience:

  * **Close Darshan** : View Lord Venkateswara from just 5 feet away (called Viswaroopa Darshan)
  * **Peaceful Atmosphere** : Limited crowd creates a calm spiritual environment
  * **Unique Blessing** : Cross the sacred Kulasekaranpadi and see the Lord in full decoration
  * **Early Access** : Be among the first devotees to see the deity each day

![Suprabhatam Seva at Tirumala – Guide to Book Your Tickets Now](/images/2025/12/Suprabhatam-Seva-at-Tirumala-%E2%80%93-Guide-to-Book-Your-Tickets-Now-1024x683.webp)Suprabhatam Seva at Tirumala – Guide to Book Your Tickets Now

**Suprabhata Seva Timings** Daily from **3:00 AM to 4:00 AM** (about 30-60 minutes). Report at **VQC-I (Garudazhvar Mandapam)** by **2:00 AM** or earlier. No seva during Dhanurmasa (Tiruppavai instead).

**Suprabhata Seva Cost** Only **Rs. 120 per person** (some sources say Rs. 240 for 2, but official is low – confirm on TTD site). Max 2 persons per booking.

**Can We Book Suprabhata Seva Online? Yes!** Yes, fully online via TTD’s **Electronic DIP (lucky draw)** system. No direct paid booking – it’s luck-based due to high demand. Advance quota opens monthly or as per schedule (often 120 days ahead or weekly Fridays).

**Suprabhata Seva TTD How to Book / How to Book Suprabhata Seva Online** Follow these easy steps on the official site:

  1. Visit **[https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/)** (only official TTD portal – avoid fakes!).
  2. Register or log in with mobile/email (new users sign up first).
  3. Go to **Pilgrim Services > Seva Electronic DIP** (or Lucky Dip section).
  4. Accept terms, fill details (name, ID proof like Aadhaar, DOB, mobile).
  5. Select **Suprabhata Seva** , choose preferred dates (multiple dates boost chances!).
  6. Submit registration and save number.
  7. Wait for lucky draw results (SMS/email if selected).
  8. If lucky, pay online quickly (within days) to confirm.

Registration often opens on Fridays (11 AM–5 PM) or specific dates – check site or TTD app for current quota (e.g., March 2026 released recently).

**How to Apply for Suprabhata Seva** Just register in the Electronic DIP during open window. One user gets only one seva in 180 days. Results via computerized draw – fair and transparent.

**Quick Tips for Success**

  * Register early and pick many dates to improve luck.
  * Keep ID proof ready (mandatory).
  * Download TTD app for alerts.
  * If not selected, try next month or offline lucky dip in Tirumala (limited 50 tickets).
  * Dress traditionally, reach early, no phones/photos inside.

Many devotees get this blessing after a few tries – don’t give up! Planning Tirumala trip? Start with **suprabhata seva online booking** on TTD site. Jai Venkateswara! May Lord Balaji select you soon

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can infants participate in Suprabhatam Seva?** Yes, infants are allowed.

**Q: How much time before should I book?** Bookings usually open 2-3 months in advance through the DIP system.

**Q: What if I’m not selected in the lucky draw?** You can register again in the next booking window or opt for other darshan types.

**Q: Is photography allowed during the seva?** No, photography and mobile phones are not permitted inside the temple.

**Q: Can I change my booking date?** No, once confirmed, dates cannot be changed. You’d need to book fresh.
