### What is 1008 Thila Homam?

Thila Homam (or Thila Havana) involves offering black sesame seeds (thila) into the sacred fire while chanting mantras to appease ancestors’ souls. The “1008” refers to the Japa Sankhya (chant count) — higher counts like 2008, 3008, 5008, or 10,008 are done for severe Dosha based on Kundali analysis.

It is usually combined with **Narayana Bali Pooja** , **Pinda Pradhan** , and **Tripindi Shraddh**. Performed by 5–12 experienced priests (depending on package), the ritual lasts 3–4 hours. It’s ideal for those with Pitru Dosha from unnatural deaths, improper last rites, or ancestral displeasure.
