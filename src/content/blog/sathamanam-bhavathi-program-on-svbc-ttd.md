Want to receive divine blessings from Lord Venkateswara on your special occasions like birthdays, wedding anniversaries, Sashtipoorthi (60th birthday), or Gruhapravesham (housewarming)? **Sathamanam Bhavathi** (also called Shatamanam Bhavati or Satamanam Bhavathi) is a heartwarming daily program on **SVBC TTD** (Sri Venkateswara Bhakti Channel) by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD). It broadcasts personalized greetings, photos, and sacred blessings from Lord Balaji to devotees celebrating milestones, making their day auspicious under Venkateswara’s grace.

### What is Sathamanam Bhavathi Program?

This devotional segment features:

  * Devotees’ names, photos, and occasion details displayed on screen.
  * Sacred chants, namavali, and blessings invoking Lord Venkateswara.
  * Messages of longevity (“Shatamanam Bhavati” meaning “May you live a hundred years”), health, prosperity, and family harmony.
  * It’s a way to share joy with the global Telugu devotional community while receiving Lord’s anugraha.

Aired daily on SVBC Telugu (and sometimes Tamil channels), it’s popular among devotees from Nellore and across Andhra Pradesh/Telangana.

### Timings and Schedule

  * **Daily Telecast Time** : 8:00 AM – 9:00 AM (or 8:30 AM – 9:00 AM in some schedules; confirm via live stream).
  * **Channel** : SVBC TTD Telugu HD (main), also on SVBC Tamil/Kannada variants.
  * **Frequency** : Every day, including special occasions.
  * In 2026, it continues as a regular morning slot (e.g., often at 10:30 PM reruns in some international listings, but primary is morning IST).

Watch live for real-time blessings — perfect for your special day!

### How to Participate and Get Your Wishes Aired

  1. **Online Submission (Recommended)** : 
     * Visit official SVBC site: **svbcttd.com/wishes.html**.
     * Fill the form: Mandatory fields include name, occasion (birthday/wedding anniversary/Sashtipoorthi/Gruhapravesham), date, photo (max 10MB), and message.
     * Start with “Om Namo Venkatesaya” and submit.
     * Submit 15–21 days in advance for processing.
  2. **SMS Method** (Older/Alternative): 
     * Type **SB** (space) then details (e.g., name, occasion, date).
     * Send to **5499979** (from any mobile).
     * Send 2–3 weeks early; include photo/details if possible.
  3. **Postal/Email** : 
     * Send photo/details to: Satamanam Bhavathi, Sri Venkateswara Bhakti Channel, Alipiri, Tirupati – 517507, Andhra Pradesh.
     * Contact: 0877-2264419 / 2264423 or 9000222603 for queries.

  * **Tips** : Include clear photo and accurate details; program focuses on auspicious events; no fee — it’s a free devotional service.
