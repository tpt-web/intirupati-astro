## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Why choose Varahaswami Guest House over other accommodations?

It offers peaceful surroundings away from the main crowd, perfect for pilgrims seeking quiet meditation and rest.

Q2: Is Varahaswami Guest House suitable for elderly pilgrims?

Yes, the peaceful environment is good for elderly pilgrims, but note the 15-20 minute walk to main temple.

Q3: How safe is the Varahaswami area at night?

Very safe with 24/7 TTD security. Well-lit pathways and regular security patrols ensure safety.

Q4: Are there any dining options nearby?

Limited options in immediate area. Main dining facilities are near temple. You can use Annadanam or carry food.

Q5: Can I visit Varahaswami Temple from the guest house?

Yes, it’s very close – just 2-3 minutes walk. Many pilgrims visit before proceeding to main temple.

Q6: What are the transportation options from this guest house?

Free TTD shuttle buses connect to main temple area. Frequency is every 15-20 minutes during day.

Q7: Is there a minimum stay requirement?

No minimum stay, but bookings are for 24-hour periods. You can book for single or multiple nights.

Q8: Can I get a room with temple view?

Some rooms may offer partial temple views, but cannot be guaranteed. Request during booking if available.

Q9: What if I have medical emergency during stay?

Immediate assistance available at reception. SVIMS hospital is 10 minutes away by TTD ambulance.

Q10: Are pets allowed in Varahaswami Guest House?

No, pets are strictly not allowed in any TTD accommodations due to temple regulations.

Q11: Can I store valuable items safely?

Lockers are available at reception for valuable items. Rooms have basic locking systems.

Q12: What’s the cancellation policy for advance bookings?

Cancellations allowed up to 24 hours before check-in. 50% refund within 24 hours, no refund for no-shows.
