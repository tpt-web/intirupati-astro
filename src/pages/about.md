**Intirupati.in** is a devotional and informational website dedicated to Tirupati, Tirumala, and other famous temples across India. Our goal is to share useful, easy-to-understand information about temple history, darshan details, festivals, traditions, and spiritual significance. We also publish temple-related images and updates to help devotees plan their visits and deepen their devotion. All content on this site is created with respect, faith, and a genuine intention to serve devotees.

**We are not associated with Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanam(TTD). We don’t accept any donations on this site.**
