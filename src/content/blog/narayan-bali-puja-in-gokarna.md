Struggling with unexplained obstacles, delayed marriage, infertility, financial losses, health issues, or family discord? These could be signs of **Pitru Dosha** or issues from ancestors who passed away unnaturally (e.g., accident, suicide, murder, snake bite, drowning, curse, or untimely death). **Narayan Bali Puja** (also called Narayana Bali or Narayanbali Puja) in Gokarna is a powerful Vedic ritual from the Garuda Purana to liberate such unsettled souls, grant them moksha, and remove ancestral curses for peace and prosperity in your life.

Gokarna, revered as “Dakshin Kashi” or the “Southern Varanasi” with the sacred Atmalinga at Mahabaleshwar Temple, is one of the most auspicious places (along with Gaya) for Pitru Karya and this puja.

### What is Narayan Bali Puja?

This ritual appeases Lord Vishnu/Narayan to fulfill the unsatisfied desires of departed souls trapped due to unnatural death or improper rites. It involves Sankalp, invoking the soul, offerings like pinda (rice balls), homam with mantras (including Narayan Bali Homam, Tila Homam), Dash Pind Pradhan, and daan to Brahmins. Often combined with related rituals like Tripindi Shraddh or Nagabali for complete Dosha Nivaran. Performed by 5+ experienced priests, it lasts 2–3.5 hours (sometimes up to 6 hours in detailed versions).

### Benefits of Narayan Bali Puja

  * Removes Pitru Dosha and ancestral curses completely
  * Grants moksha/peace to the departed soul, freeing them from earthly bonds
  * Eliminates life obstacles: delayed marriage, childbirth issues, career/financial blocks
  * Brings family harmony, mental peace, good health, and protection from negative energies
  * Ensures success in endeavors, prosperity, and positive vibes for descendants
  * Ends recurring bad dreams, misfortunes, or unfulfilled ancestral desires

Highly effective when done in Gokarna’s holy ambiance.

Report early (head bath required); fast during puja.

  * Abstain from non-veg, alcohol 3–11 days before/after (longer if recent death).
