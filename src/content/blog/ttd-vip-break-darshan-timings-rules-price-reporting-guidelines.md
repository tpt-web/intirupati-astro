For devotees seeking a serene and intimate darshan of Lord Venkateswara at Tirumala, the **VIP Break Darshan** is a highly sought-after option. Conducted during a quiet interval in the evening before the main temple opens to the general public, it offers a calm and less crowded spiritual experience. This guide explains the essential timings, strict reporting guidelines, and the primary booking method through the Srivani Trust.

#### **What is VIP Break Darshan?**

The VIP Break Darshan takes place in the “break” between temple rituals during the evening hours. It allows a limited number of devotees to enter the sanctum sanctorum in an orderly and peaceful manner. Due to its limited slots, it is considered one of the most time-efficient and tranquil darshan experiences in Tirumala.

#### **Key Timings and Reporting Guidelines**

Adhering to the schedule and rules is mandatory for a successful darshan.

  * **General Timings:**
    * **Reporting Time:** Around **3:30 PM** at the designated location.
    * **Darshan Time:** Typically begins between **4:00 PM to 5:00 PM**.
    * **Important Note:** The **timings printed on your official Srivani Trust ticket are final** and must be followed strictly. Late arrivals are not permitted.
  * **Essential Reporting Guidelines:**
    1. **Documents:** You must carry your **printed/digital Srivani VIP Break Darshan ticket** , the **original photo ID proof** used for booking, and your donation receipt.
    2. **Dress Code:** A traditional dress code is strictly enforced. 
       * **Men:** Dhoti with angavastram, or formal trousers with a shirt.
       * **Women:** Saree, salwar kameez, or a long skirt with a dupatta.
       * Jeans, shorts, T-shirts, and sleeveless tops are **not allowed**.
    3. **At the Temple:** Report to **Vaikuntham Queue Complex – I** (as per your ticket), deposit mobile phones where instructed, and follow all queue discipline and staff guidance. Photography is prohibited.

#### **[How to Book VIP Break Darshan](/how-to-book-vip-darshan-ttd-srivani-trust-donations-e-donations-slabs-benefits-details/): The Srivani Trust Route**

The most transparent and official method to secure VIP Break Darshan tickets is through the **TTD’s Srivani Trust**.

  * **Eligibility:** A minimum donation of **₹10,000** to the Srivani Trust makes a devotee eligible to book a **TTD VIP Darshan 10000** ticket online.
  * **Booking:** Tickets are issued per individual and are subject to availability. The online system managed by TTD ensures a fair and authorized process.

#### **Common Mistakes to Avoid**

  * Reporting late, even by a few minutes.
  * Wearing casual or prohibited attire.
  * Forgetting original ID proof or ticket.
  * Carrying mobile phones or other prohibited items into the darshan queue.

A: The rules are very strict. Devotees who report late, even with a confirmed ticket, are typically not permitted to join the darshan. It is crucial to plan your arrival in Tirumala with ample buffer time.

A: There are no specific age restrictions. However, all devotees, including children, must have a valid ticket and adhere to the dress code and reporting guidelines.
