The historic **Sri Vedavalli Sameta Sri Vedanarayana Swamy Temple** (Matsya Narayana Perumal Temple) in Nagalapuram, managed by **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)** , is set for a major revival. On **February 6, 2026** , TTD Executive Officer Sri M. Ravichandra released the official posters announcing the **Vimana Jeernoddharana** (tower renovation) and **Ashtabandana Mahasamprokshanam**.

### Temple Highlights & Significance

  * **Legend** : Lord Vishnu as Matsya Avatar rescued the Vedas from demon Somakasura in the ocean.
  * **Unique Feature** : Annual **Surya Pooja** – Sunlight directly touches the Lord’s feet during Brahmotsavam.
  * **Architecture** : Vijayanagara-style with intricate carvings and serene gardens.
  * **Blessings Sought** : Education, wisdom, health, and protection.
