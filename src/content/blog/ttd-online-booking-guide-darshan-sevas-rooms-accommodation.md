Planning a Tirumala trip in 2026? This easy guide explains **TTD online booking** on the official website ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in. Book **Rs 300 darshan tickets** , **TTD rooms online** , **TTD sevas** and more without stress. All info is simple and up-to-date for 2026.

### What You Can Book Online with TTD in 2026

  * **Rs 300 Special Entry Darshan** – Fast darshan + 2 free laddus
  * **TTD accommodation** – Rooms in Tirumala and Tirupati
  * **TTD sevas** – Like Kalyanotsavam, Suprabhata Seva (often lucky dip)
  * Donations (e-Hundi) and more

Always book only on official site: [ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/) Avoid fake sites!

### How to Book Rs 300 Darshan Tickets Online in 2026

This is the most popular ticket for quick darshan.

**Simple steps:**

  1. Go to ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in
  2. Register or login with mobile + Aadhaar
  3. Select “Special Entry Darshan”
  4. Choose your date (quotas open monthly, e.g., April 2026 opened in Jan)
  5. Pick time slot
  6. Add up to 6 people
  7. Pay ₹300 per person
  8. Download and print ticket

Report 30 minutes early at ATC Parking. Wear traditional clothes.

**Tip:** Quotas finish fast – login at opening time (like 10 AM or 3 PM). For more on darshan: Check our [Suprabhata Seva guide](/suprabhatam-seva-at-tirumala-guide-to-book-your-tickets-now/)

### [TTD Accommodation Online Booking](https://intirupati.in/category/tirupati/accommodations/) (Rooms in Tirumala & Tirupati)

Book paid rooms 1–90 days ahead. Only one room per user in 90 days.

**Room types & prices (approx):**

  * Basic: ₹100–500
  * Good/deluxe: ₹1000–4000+

**Steps to book:**

  1. Login to TTD site
  2. Go to “[Accommodation](https://intirupati.in/category/tirupati/accommodations/)”
  3. Select Tirumala or Tirupati
  4. Choose dates and room type (min 2 persons often)
  5. Pay rent + deposit
  6. Print confirmation

**Tip:** No Tirumala room? Book Tirupati – free buses go up the hill. Free rooms available on arrival at CRO (long wait in peak time). Read our full Tirumala rooms guide

### How to Book TTD Seva Tickets Online in 2026

Sevas give close darshan and special blessings.

**Popular sevas & approx prices:**

  * [Kalyanotsavam](/tirumala-kalyanotsavam-seva-booking-guide-timings-tickets-darshan-cost/): ₹1,200
  * Sahasra Kalasabhishekam: ₹1,200
  * Vasanthotsavam: ₹500
  * [Suprabhata Seva](/suprabhatam-seva-at-tirumala-guide-to-book-your-tickets-now/): ₹120 (mostly lucky dip / e-DIP)
  * **[Arjitha](/arjitha-sevas-types-booking-reporting-timing-rules-dress-code/)** Brahmotsavam: ₹5,000+

**Rules:**

  * Max 2 persons per seva
  * Only 1 seva in 180 days per user
  * Children under 12 free
  * Traditional dress required
  * Free darshan after seva

**Steps:**

  1. Login to TTD site
  2. Go to “Seva” or “E-DIP”
  3. Register for lucky dip if needed (e.g., Suprabhata opens monthly)
  4. Select date/seva
  5. Pay if selected
  6. Print ticket

Many sevas use e-DIP (lucky draw) due to high demand. For Suprabhata details: See our [Suprabhata Seva offline & online post](https://tirupatitirumalainfo.com/suprabatha-seva-in-offline-at-tirumala/)

### Common Problems & Quick Fixes

  * Tickets sold out fast? → Login at exact release time with fast internet
  * Payment failed? → Money refunds in 7–10 days
  * No rooms? → Try Tirupati or private hotels
  * Booking not transferable → ID must match the registered person

### FAQ – TTD Online Booking 2026 (One-Line Q&A)

**Q: How to do TTD online booking 2026?** A: Register on ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in, login, select darshan/seva/rooms, pay and download ticket.

**Q: How to book Rs 300 darshan tickets online?** A: Login, choose Special Entry Darshan, pick date/slot, pay ₹300 per person (up to 6).

**Q: When do TTD quotas open for 2026?** A: Monthly – e.g., March 2026 sevas released Jan 27 at 3 PM; check site for exact dates.

**Q: Can I book TTD accommodation online?** A: Yes, 1–90 days ahead; only one room per user in 90 days.

**Q: How to book TTD seva tickets online?** A: Use Seva or E-DIP section; many need lucky dip registration first.

**Q: Is Suprabhata Seva bookable online?** A: Yes, via e-DIP lucky draw (register monthly); limited spots.

**Q: What if TTD tickets are sold out?** A: Try next month quota, linked homam tickets, or offline in Tirumala.

**Q: Is TTD booking transferable?** A: No – original ID holder must attend.

**Q: What is the cost of TTD rooms?** A: ₹100 to ₹4000+ per day depending on type.

**Q: Where to check TTD latest updates?** A: Official site ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in or TTD app.
