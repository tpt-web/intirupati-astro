The Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) manages a vast network of temples beyond the main hill shrine. These sacred sites, spread across Andhra Pradesh and India, each have their own unique spiritual significance and daily schedules. This guide provides a clear list of major TTD temples with their opening status and timings, helping you plan your visits seamlessly.

**Key Tip for Pilgrims:** Timings are subject to change during festivals, eclipses, or special rituals. For the most accurate and current information, always verify with the **official TTD website** or contact the local temple administration before your visit.
