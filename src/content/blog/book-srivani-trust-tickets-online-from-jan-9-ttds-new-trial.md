Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) is making it easier to book Srivani Trust tickets. From **January 9** , devotees can book their tickets online for the first time. This is a trial run of the new system.

**What is the Srivani Trust?**  
The Srivani Trust is a donation scheme. When devotees donate, they receive a receipt and a ticket that allows special darshan of Lord Venkateswara in Tirumala. The donation supports TTD’s charity and religious activities.

**What’s changing?**  
Until now, Srivani tickets were issued mainly through manual or offline methods. Starting **Tuesday, January 9, 2024** , TTD is testing an **online current booking facility**. This means you can book your ticket from home before traveling.

**Key Points of the New System:**

  * Starts: **January 9, 2024**
  * Status: Trial basis
  * Purpose: To simplify booking and reduce in-person paperwork

**Benefits of Online Booking:**

  * Book from anywhere, anytime
  * Avoid long queues at counters
  * Secure online payment
  * Instant confirmation via email/SMS
  * Better travel planning with a confirmed ticket

**How to Book Online (Expected Steps):**

  1. Visit the official TTD website: **[ttd.seva.tirumala.org](https://ttd.seva.tirumala.org/)** or **[tirumala.org](https://tirumala.org/)**
  2. Look for **“[Srivani Trust Online Booking](/how-to-book-srivani-darshan-tickets-online-offline/)”** or “Current Booking”
  3. Select your preferred darshan date
  4. Enter donor and pilgrim details
  5. Make payment online
  6. Download/save your e-receipt and ticket confirmation

> [How to Book Srivani Darshan Tickets Online & Offline](/how-to-book-srivani-darshan-tickets-online-offline/)

**Important Information for Devotees:**

  * This is a **trial service** – there may be changes based on feedback
  * Use only TTD’s official websites to avoid fraud
  * Keep your e-receipt safe for verification in Tirumala
  * Follow all TTD dress code and temple rules during your visit

TTD hopes this new facility will make the pilgrimage experience smoother. Devotees are encouraged to use the online system and share their feedback to help improve the service.

_For exact steps and updates, check the TTD website or news portal after January 9._
