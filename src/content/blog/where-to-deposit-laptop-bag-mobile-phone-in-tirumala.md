In **Tirumala** at the Sri Venkateswara Temple (Tirupati Balaji), **laptop bags** , **mobile phones** , and other luggage/electronics are **strictly prohibited** inside the main temple complex, sanctum, or darshan queues to preserve sanctity and prevent distractions. TTD provides **free** mobile lockers, baggage deposit counters, and luggage facilities at key locations. These are safe, guarded (often with CCTV), and operational 24/7 at major spots—deposit before entering queues and collect after darshan with your token/receipt. For information on **Where to Deposit Laptop Bag & Mobile Phone in tirumala**, refer to the TTD guidelines.

### Why Deposit Items?

  * Mobiles, laptops, cameras, smartwatches, and electronics banned inside—no photos, calls, or use allowed.
  * Large bags, trolleys, laptop bags, and heavy luggage not permitted in darshan areas.
  * Small wallets/purses (for money, tickets) and prasadam packets usually OK; leather items (belts, bags) restricted.
  * Free depositing helps maintain focus on devotion to Lord Venkateswara.

### Key Deposit Locations & Process

TTD counters are at major entry points, queue complexes, and PACs (Pilgrim Amenities Complexes). Process: Approach counter, deposit items (tag heavy luggage), get token/slip, collect post-darshan (some have separate collection points like PAC IV for VQC deposits).

Main Locations (based on latest TTD info and devotee reports):

  * **Vaikuntam Queue Complex (VQC I & II)** — Primary for darshan queues; deposit mobiles/electronics here. Collection often at **PAC IV** (Old Annadanam Complex) after darshan.
  * **Pilgrim Amenities Complex (PAC I, PAC II, PAC III, PAC IV)** — Dedicated counters for mobiles, bags, luggage; PAC III has new central lockers (1420+ capacity). PAC IV common for collection.
  * **Sarva Darshan / Divya Darshan / Rs.300 Darshan Entrances** — Luggage and mobile counters available; convenient for general devotees.
  * **Break Darshan / Special Entries** — Deposit at luggage center next to List 2 entrance.
  * **Central Reception Office (CRO)** — Near room allotment; good for overall baggage.
  * **Kalyana Katta (Tonsure Complex)** — Separate baggage counters.
  * **Other** — Near bus stand areas, guest houses, or main gates; some free lockers at 5 locations (e.g., Madhava Nilayam/PAC 2, Padmanabha Nilayam, Yatri Nivas— Aadhaar mandatory for some lockers).

For early morning darshan: Deposit the night before or at entry to avoid rush.

### Step-by-Step Process

  1. Reach counter near your queue/entry (ask TTD staff/info desk if unsure).
  2. Deposit laptop bag, mobile, etc. (heavy items tagged).
  3. Get token/receipt—keep it safe!
  4. Proceed for darshan (dress code: traditional attire; no leather/electronics).
  5. Collect after exit from designated counter (e.g., PAC IV for VQC deposits; some transfer to exit points).
  6. Families can use one token.

Timings: 24/7 at major complexes; no charge—completely free.

### Tips for Smooth Experience

  * Deposit early (night before) for morning darshan.
  * Use official TTD counters only—avoid unofficial places.
  * For solo travelers: Free lockers available (Aadhaar needed at some).
  * Valuables like jewelry: Better keep minimal or carry securely (not recommended to deposit).
  * If walking Alipiri/Srivari Mettu: Deposit at top or use luggage drop service.

### FAQ

**Q: Are deposit facilities free?** A: Yes—completely free at all TTD counters; no charges for mobiles, bags, or luggage.

**Q: Where do I collect after darshan?** A: Often separate collection like PAC IV for VQC deposits; check token for counter number—some transfer to exit points.

**Q: Can I deposit laptop bag and mobile together?** A: Yes—at any baggage/mobile counter (e.g., VQC, PACs); tag heavy laptop bag if needed.

**Q: What if I have early morning darshan?** A: Deposit night before at PACs/VQC or upon arrival; counters open 24/7.

**Q: Are there lockers for valuables?** A: Yes—free at select spots (e.g., PAC III central lockers); Aadhaar may be required.
