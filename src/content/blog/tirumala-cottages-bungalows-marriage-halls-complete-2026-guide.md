## 📱 Booking Information

**Online Booking:** Available for some categories at ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in

**Offline Booking:** Apply at TTD Reception Office in Tirumala

**Document Requirements:** ID proof for individuals, documents for marriage halls

**Advance Period:** Varies by category (30-90 days)

[Check Availability →](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/)

**💡 Important:** Rambagicha cottages and marriage halls require special permission and longer advance booking. Contact TTD Reception Office directly for these categories.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Who can book Rambagicha cottages?

Primarily for families and groups. Special permission may be required. Government officials get priority.

Q2: What facilities are included in Rambagicha cottages?

Independent living spaces, multiple rooms, basic kitchenette in some, attached bathrooms, and more privacy.

Q3: Can common pilgrims book HUDC complex?

Limited availability for common pilgrims. Priority given to government officials on duty. Check TTD portal for public quota.

Q4: How to book Tirumala marriage halls?

Separate application to TTD’s Kalyanamandapam department. Advance booking of 3-6 months recommended.

Q5: What documents needed for marriage hall booking?

Marriage invitation, ID proofs of bride and groom, address proof, and advance payment receipt.

Q6: Are Travellers Bungalows suitable for solo pilgrims?

Yes, they’re ideal for solo travellers or couples. Basic facilities at affordable rates.

Q7: Can I cook food in cottages?

Some cottages have basic kitchen facilities, but check specific rules. Most pilgrims use Annadanam.

Q8: What’s the maximum capacity for marriage halls?

Varies from 100 to 500 people depending on the hall. Specify requirements during application.

Q9: Are these accommodations wheelchair accessible?

Some cottages and bungalows have accessibility features. Specify requirements during booking.

Q10: What is the booking lead time for cottages?

3-6 months advance booking recommended, especially for Rambagicha cottages during peak seasons.

Q11: Can foreigners book these accommodations?

Yes, with valid passport and visa documents. Some categories may have restrictions for foreign nationals.

Q12: What are the quiet hours in these areas?

10:00 PM to 6:00 AM are quiet hours. Music and loud activities are restricted during these times.

Q13: Is there 24-hour security in cottage areas?

Yes, all TTD accommodation areas have 24/7 security patrols and CCTV surveillance.

Q14: Can I get daily housekeeping in cottages?

Basic housekeeping is provided. Additional services may be available on request for extra charge.
