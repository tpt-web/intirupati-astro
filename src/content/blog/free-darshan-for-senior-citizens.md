For senior devotees visiting the sacred hill shrine of Tirumala Venkateswara Temple managed by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams, the **free darshan for senior citizens at Tirumala** is a blessed concession that makes pilgrimage comfortable.

This guide compiles **only official information** from TTD sources — ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in and tirumala.org — with the latest 2026 quota dates and rules. No lengthy narratives; just clear, structured facts to help you book and plan.

## Eligibility & Official Rules (As per TTD)

The concession is strictly for **Senior Citizens (aged 65+), Differently Abled, and specified Medical Cases**.

### Category-wise Requirements

Category| Mandatory Documents| Attendant  
---|---|---  
**Senior Citizen (65+ completed)**|  Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport (age proof)| Only spouse allowed  
**Differently Abled**|  Disability certificate (State/Central Govt.)| Only spouse allowed  
**Medical Cases** (Kidney/liver transplant, open heart surgery, dialysis, cancer, brain/spine surgery unable to walk)| Valid medical certificate from concerned surgeon/specialist| Only spouse allowed  
  
### Important Rules

  * **Age requirement:** Must have completed 65 years on the date of darshan.
  * **Attendant:** Only **spouse** is permitted as free attendant. No children or relatives.
  * **Daily quota:** 1,000 senior citizens per day (Monday–Saturday).
  * **Darshan timing:** Monday to Saturday at **3:00 PM** (confirm timing on ticket).
  * **Cool-off period:** 90 days mandatory between two senior citizen darshans.
  * **Dress code:** Traditional attire only.
  * **No offline booking:** Only online via official portal.

## Step-by-Step Online Booking (Official Portal)

Book only through the official government portal:

👉 [https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/)

### Booking Steps

  1. Visit the portal and login using mobile OTP.
  2. Under “Pilgrim Services”, select **“Differently Abled / Sr. Citizen / Medical Cases”**.
  3. Choose an available darshan date.
  4. Enter senior citizen and spouse details.
  5. Upload clear age proof document.
  6. Submit and download the darshan ticket.

⚠️ **Important:**  
There is **no payment**. The darshan is completely free. Avoid unofficial websites.

## Reporting Place & Time on Darshan Day

  * **Reporting Place:** Senior Citizen Hall, South Mada Street (near Supatham entry)
  * **Reporting Time:** 2 hours before darshan time  
For 3:00 PM darshan, report by **1:00 PM**
  * **Facilities Available:** Food, water, milk, and free wheelchair assistance

## Required Documents Checklist

Carry **original documents**. Digital copies are not accepted at entry.

  * Original Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport (with DOB)
  * Spouse original ID proof
  * Medical certificate (if applicable)
  * Disability certificate (if applicable)
  * Printed or mobile darshan ticket
