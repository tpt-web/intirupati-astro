**Quick Guide:** Skip long queues at Tirumala with Srivani VIP Break Darshan! Book online at TTD portal or get same-day tickets offline. Donation: ₹10,500 per person. Read our complete booking guide below.

## What is Srivani Darshan?

Srivani VIP Break Darshan is a special priority darshan offered by Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) for devotees who donate to the **Srivani Trust** (Sri Venkateswara Aalayala Nirmanam Trust). This darshan allows pilgrims to have quick access to Lord Venkateswara without waiting in regular queues.

**Key Benefits:**

  * Fast-track entry during break hours
  * Stand 5-10 feet from the deity
  * Darshan time: 1-2 minutes at first door
  * Less waiting compared to general darshan
  * Support Hindu Dharma activities

## Srivani Darshan Booking Cost 

  * **Total Amount:** ₹10,500 per person 
    * Donation to Srivani Trust: ₹10,000
    * Darshan ticket: ₹500
  * **Children below 12 years:** Free (can accompany parents)
  * **Tax benefit:** Eligible for 80G deduction under Income Tax Act

## Online Booking Process (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Visit Official TTD Website

Go to [https://tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in](https://tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in/) or [https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in](https://ttdevasthanams.ap.gov.in/)

### Step 2: Register/Login

  * Sign up with mobile number
  * Verify through OTP
  * Complete profile details

### Step 3: Make Srivani Trust Donation

  * Click on **“Donations”** tab
  * Select **“Srivani Trust”**
  * Enter ₹10,000 per person
  * Complete online payment (Credit/Debit Card/Net Banking)
  * Save donation receipt

### Step 4: Check Darshan Availability

After donation confirmation:

  * Go to **Services → Srivani Donor Darshan**
  * Check availability calendar
  * Select preferred date (subject to quota)

### Step 5: Book VIP Break Darshan

  * Choose available time slot
  * Pay ₹500 darshan fee
  * Download e-ticket and donation receipt

**Pro Tip:** Srivani tickets get booked fast! Check availability before making donation to avoid disappointment.

## Offline Booking Process (Same Day Tickets)

### Counter Locations

**1\. Srivani Ticket Counter, Tirumala**

  * Location: Opposite Annamayya Bhavan, Tirumala
  * Daily quota: 800 tickets
  * Opening time: 7:00 AM
  * Tip: Queue forms 1-2 hours before opening

**2\. Tirupati Airport Counter**

  * Location: Tirupati Airport premises
  * Daily quota: 200 tickets
  * Opening time: 7:00 AM
  * Eligibility: Only for flight passengers (boarding pass required)

### Offline Booking Requirements

  * Original photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport/PAN Card)
  * 3 photocopies of ID proof
  * Cash/Card for payment (₹10,500 per person)
  * Personal presence mandatory

**Important Note:** Offline counters at Tirupati Airport and Tirumala are currently closed until January 9, 2026. Check TTD WhatsApp service for real-time availability updates.

## FAQ – Srivani Darshan Booking

**Q1. Can I book Srivani Darshan without donation?**  
No. A minimum ₹10,000 donation to Srivani Trust is mandatory for each person.

**Q2. Is Srivani Darshan worth ₹10,000?**  
Yes, if you want quick darshan with minimal waiting. Regular Sarva Darshan can take 8-20 hours.

**Q3. Can I book for my entire family?**  
Yes, but each person needs separate ₹10,000 donation and ₹500 ticket.

**Q4. What if slots are unavailable after donation?**  
You can try booking on another date within the quota period. Donation remains valid.

**Q5. Is recommendation letter needed?**  
No recommendation required for Srivani Trust donors. Only separate VIP darshan needs recommendations.

**Q6. Can I get refund if I miss darshan?**  
No. Donations are non-refundable even if you miss the darshan slot.

**Q7. How to check Srivani ticket availability?**  
Use TTD WhatsApp service or check online portal before visiting counters.

**Q8. Can foreigners book Srivani Darshan?**  
Yes, Indian citizens, NRIs, and foreign nationals can all book with valid ID proof.

**Q9. Are there agent services for Srivani booking?**  
TTD recommends booking directly. Many travel agencies offer package assistance but don’t buy from unauthorized agents.

**Q10. What’s the difference between Srivani and Special Entry Darshan?**  
Srivani requires ₹10,500 donation, no advance booking needed for offline. Special Entry costs ₹300, requires advance online booking.
