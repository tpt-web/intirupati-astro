Finding the right place to stay is a crucial part of your Tirumala pilgrimage. While the TTD runs its own guest houses, many devotees prefer the spiritual simplicity and community atmosphere of **mutts (monasteries) and dharmashalas (charitable lodges)**. These options are not only budget-friendly but often place you within walking distance of the sacred temple.

This guide provides a comprehensive, verified list of mutt accommodations with their phone numbers, along with practical tips to help you secure your stay for a focused and serene spiritual journey.

## 🛌 Understanding Your Accommodation Options in Tirumala/Tirupati

Tirumala offers a range of stays to suit different needs and preferences:

  1. **Mutts & Dharmashalas (Spiritual & Budget)**: As listed above, these are ideal for devotees seeking simplicity and a devotional atmosphere. They are very affordable, but facilities are basic (shared bathrooms, simple furnishings). **Booking is typically done by direct phone call** on a first-come, first-served basis.
  2. **TTD-Run Guest Houses (Book in Advance)** : For more structured comfort, TTD operates several guest houses like **Srinivasam** , **Vishnu Nivasam** , **Sri Padmavathi Guest House** , and **Madhavam**. **Advance online booking via the TTD portal is essential** due to high demand. Detailed guides for each are available on our website, **[intirupati.in](/)**.
  3. **Private Hotels & Lodges (For Amenities)**: Several private establishments near the bus stand and main roads offer rooms with more amenities (attached bathrooms, room service). These can be booked online through travel websites.

## ✅ Essential Tips for Booking Your Stay

  * **Call Well in Advance** : Start contacting mutts **days or even weeks before your trip** , especially if traveling during weekends, festivals, or holidays.
  * **Be Prepared with Details** : When you call, have your planned dates, the number of people in your group, and their names ready.
  * **Confirm Everything** : Clearly ask about the total cost (if any), check-in/check-out times, and any specific rules (e.g., curfews, meal availability).
  * **Have a Backup List** : Mutts fill up quickly. Have the contact numbers for 3-5 preferred options ready to call in sequence.
  * **For TTD Rooms** : If you haven’t booked online, you can try the **Central Reception Office (CRO)** in Tirumala upon arrival for any last-minute cancellations, but this is not guaranteed.
  * **Complete Your Pilgrimage Plan** : For a seamless trip, use our comprehensive resources on **[intirupati.in](/)** to plan your **darshan, seva bookings, and understand temple rituals**.
