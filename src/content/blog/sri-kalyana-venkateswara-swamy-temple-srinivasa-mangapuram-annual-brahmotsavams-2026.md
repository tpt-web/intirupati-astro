The sacred **Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy Temple** in Srinivasa Mangapuram, just 12 km west of Tirupati and managed by **Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)** , is gearing up for its grand annual **Brahmotsavams**. This ancient shrine, revered next only to Tirumala for marital bliss and family harmony, holds special significance for newlyweds as the place where Lord Venkateswara and Goddess Padmavathi stayed post-marriage.

Recent TTD news highlights the preparatory **Koil Alwar Tirumanjanam** performed on February 5, 2026, ahead of the festivities.

### Event Overview

Aspect| Details  
---|---  
Temple Name| Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy Temple, Srinivasa Mangapuram  
Main Festival| Annual Brahmotsavams (Brahmotsavam)  
Announcement/Prep Date| Koil Alwar Tirumanjanam: February 5, 2026  
Ankurarpanam| February 7, 2026 (Evening)  
Main Festival Dates| February 8 to 16, 2026  
Organizer| Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD)  
Significance| Auspicious celebrations with Vahana Sevas, rituals for blessings in marriage, family, and prosperity  
  
Devotees flock here for a serene alternative to Tirumala, especially couples seeking divine marital blessings.

Note: Exact daily Vahana Seva timings and specific rituals (e.g., Dwajarohanam on first day) follow traditional patterns—check TTD official channels for precise schedule as events unfold.

### Temple Highlights & Significance

  * **Legend & Importance**: Lord Venkateswara (as Kalyana Venkateswara) with consorts Sridevi & Bhudevi; ideal for newlyweds fulfilling vows.
  * **Architecture & Maintenance**: Ancient temple under ASI, maintained by TTD since 1967; beautiful setting with divine aura.
  * **Attractions** : Grand Vahana Sevas during Brahmotsavams, peaceful darshan compared to main Tirumala crowds.
  * **Blessings Sought** : Marriage harmony, family welfare, fulfillment of wishes.
